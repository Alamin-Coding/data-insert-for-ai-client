Question:
How did Raju's experience during the school fire influence his career choice?

Options:


Correct Answer: He became a firefighter after being inspired by the firefighters who helped during the fire.

Solution:


Explanation:
রজু স্কুলের অগ্নিকাণ্ডে দমকলকর্মীদের সাহস, দ্রুততা ও মানুষকে বাঁচাতে তাদের কাজ সরাসরি দেখেছিল, যা তার মনে শক্ত প্রভাব ফেলেছিল। সেই অনুপ্রেরণায় তিনি অন্যদের সাহায্য করার জন্য এবং একই ধরনের সাহসিকতা প্রদর্শনের লক্ষ্যে ফায়ারফাইটার হওয়ার সিদ্ধান্ত নেন।

Question:
How does Raju's experience during a school fire influence his decision to become a firefighter, and how does this relate to his later role in teaching fire safety to children?

Options:


Correct Answer: Raju's experience during the fire made him appreciate the importance of firefighting and safety, which led him to teach children about fire safety as part of his job.

Solution:


Explanation:
Raju’s firsthand experience of the school fire made him see how dangerous fires are and how important firefighters and safety measures can be, so he chose to become a firefighter. Later, drawing on that experience, he teaches children fire safety to help them avoid danger and to share practical lessons about prevention and response.
