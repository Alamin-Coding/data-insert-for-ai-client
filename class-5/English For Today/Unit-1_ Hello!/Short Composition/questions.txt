Question:
Write a short composition on "A Journey by Train" [Write at least five sentences to the topic. Remember to use capital letters, punctuation, correct spelling and sentence structure.]

Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:
Last summer, I went on a train journey with my family. We traveled from Dhaka to Chattogram to visit my grandparents. The train moved quickly, and I enjoyed watching the green fields and rivers outside the window. I also ate some snacks and talked with my parents during the ride. It was a wonderful experience, and I will always remember this joyful journey.
