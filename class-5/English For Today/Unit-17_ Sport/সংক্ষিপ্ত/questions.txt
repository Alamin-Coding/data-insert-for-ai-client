Question:
How often are the Olympic Games held?

Options:


Correct Answer: Every four years.

Solution:
Solution:
Approach — Identify the recurrence pattern of the Olympic Games and note any modern scheduling details.

Steps and reasoning:
1. The Olympic Games follow a quadrennial cycle, meaning each edition of the Games is scheduled once every four years.
2. This follows the ancient Olympic tradition and was adopted by the modern Olympics (revived in 1896).
3. Note the modern scheduling nuance: both the Summer and Winter Olympics are each held every four years. Since 1994 the Summer and Winter Games have been staggered so that there is an Olympic Games every two years, but each specific Games (Summer or Winter) still occurs every four years.
4. Examples: Summer Olympics — 2012, 2016, 2020 (held in 2021 due to the pandemic), 2024; Winter Olympics — 2010, 2014, 2018, 2022.

Answer: The Olympic Games are held every four years.

Explanation:
The Olympic Games are held every four years. This quadrennial schedule follows the ancient tradition and gives enough time for countries to prepare host facilities and for athletes to train and qualify.

Question:
What is a significant characteristic of the Olympic Games?

Options:


Correct Answer: More than 200 nations compete.

Solution:
Solution:
Answer approach — identify a defining feature of the Olympic Games and explain its significance.

Step 1 — State the characteristic:
A key characteristic of the Olympic Games is that they bring together athletes from more than 200 nations (represented by over 200 National Olympic Committees).

Step 2 — Relevant concepts and evidence:
- Universality: The International Olympic Committee recognizes and works with National Olympic Committees from most countries and territories, so the Games have very wide global representation.
- Inclusiveness: The large number of participating nations shows the Olympics are a truly global sporting event, not limited to one region or culture.

Step 3 — Logical reasoning and significance:
- Because over 200 nations participate, the Olympics promote international cooperation, cultural exchange, and peaceful competition on a worldwide scale.
- This wide participation increases the Games’ visibility, competitiveness, and diplomatic importance, distinguishing the Olympics from many other sporting events.

Concise conclusion:
Therefore, a significant characteristic of the Olympic Games is that more than 200 nations compete, reflecting their global reach and unifying role.

Explanation:
The Olympic Games are a global sporting event in which over 200 National Olympic Committees send athletes to compete. This large number of participating nations demonstrates the universality and international character of the Olympics.

Question:
What activity does Anousha enjoy doing on weekends?

Options:


Correct Answer: Playing badminton.

Solution:
সমাধান:
পদ্ধতি:
1. প্রথমে দেওয়া পাঠ্য বা প্রশ্নটি সাবধানে পড়ে দেখুন যাতে "on weekends" বা বাংলা হলে "সপ্তাহান্তে" শব্দটি কোথায় ব্যবহার হয়েছে তা শনাক্ত করা যায়।  
2. এরপর সেই বাক্যে থাকা ক্রিয়া বা অনুরাগ দেখুন — যেমন enjoys, likes, loves — যা ব্যক্তি কোন কাজটি করতে পছন্দ করেন তা নির্দেশ করে।  
3. সেই তথ্য থেকে সরাসরি উপসংহার করুন কোন কার্যকলাপটি সপ্তাহান্তে করা হয়।

প্রমাণ ও যুক্তি:
- পাঠ্যে যেখানে বলা আছে যে Anousha "enjoys doing" বা "সপ্তাহান্তে তিনি উপভোগ করেন"—এর মতো শব্দ ব্যবহার করা হয়েছে, সেখানে যে ক্রিয়াটি উল্লেখ আছে সেটিই তার প্রিয় সপ্তাহান্তের কার্যকলাপ।  
- সেই ক্রিয়াটি হল ব্যাডমিন্টন খেলা, তাই সরাসরি ও স্পষ্টভাবে বলা যায় যে তিনি সপ্তাহান্তে ব্যাডমিন্টন খেলতে পছন্দ করেন।

উত্তর:
উনি সপ্তাহান্তে ব্যাডমিন্টন খেলা পছন্দ করেন।

Explanation:
Anousha enjoys playing badminton on weekends because the passage explicitly states that she spends her weekends playing badminton. To answer this type of question, identify the direct statement in the text that names her weekend activity.
