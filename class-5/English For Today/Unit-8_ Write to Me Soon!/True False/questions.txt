Question:
Sima is unable to go to school because she feels well and wants to stay at home.

Options:
a. False
b. True


Correct Answer: ক

Solution:


Explanation:
বিবৃতিটি ভুল — "unable to go to school" মানে যেতে অক্ষম হওয়া, কিন্তু "feels well and wants to stay at home" হলে সে সুস্থ ও ইচ্ছাকৃতভাবে বাড়িতে থাকতে চায়, যা অক্ষমতার কারণ নয়; তাই বিবৃতিটি অসঙ্গত ও False।

Question:
Sima's doctor advised her to eat even if she is not hungry, as it will help her regain strength while she is sick.

Options:
a. True
b. False


Correct Answer: ক

Solution:


Explanation:
True — when someone is sick the body needs extra energy and nutrients to fight infection and repair tissues, so eating (even small amounts) helps maintain strength and prevent muscle loss. If appetite is low, doctors often recommend frequent small meals, nutrient-dense foods, and adequate fluids to support recovery.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Question:
Sima's doctor advised her to avoid eating food even when she doesn’t feel hungry.

Options:
a. True
b. False


Correct Answer: খ

Solution:


Explanation:
The statement is false because doctors do not generally tell patients to avoid eating simply because they are not hungry; medical advice depends on the person's condition and needs. In most cases a doctor would give specific instructions (e.g., skip food before tests or follow a diet) rather than a blanket rule to avoid eating when not hungry.
