Question:
Write a short composition about a competition you attended at school.

[Write at least five sentences to the topic. Remember to use capital letters, punctuation, correct spelling and sentence structure.]

Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:
I attended a race competition held in our school this year. It was a 100 metre race. There were six participants including me. Our game teacher whistled and we started running. I tried hard but won the second prize. However, I was happy.
