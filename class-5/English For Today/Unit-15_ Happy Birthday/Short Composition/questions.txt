Question:
Write a short composition about your birthday party. [Write at least five sentences to the topic. Remember to use capital letters, punctuation, correct spelling and sentence structure.]

Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:
I arranged a birthday party on January 10, which is my birthday. I invited my friends to the party. We sang songs, played games and had a lot of fun. While cutting cake, they wished me singing &quot;Happy birthday to you&quot;. I got many nice gifts from my parents and friends.
