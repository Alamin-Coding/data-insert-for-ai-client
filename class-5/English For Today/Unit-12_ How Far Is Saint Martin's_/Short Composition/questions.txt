Question:
Write a short composition on "Some tourist spots in Bangladesh".

Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:
There are many tourist spots in Bangladesh. Cox's Bazar, Saint Martin's Island, Srimangal, Madhabkundu, etc are some of them. One can enjoy the sea and the beach in Cox's Bazar. Srimangal is famous for beautiful tea gardens and Madhabkundu for waterfalls. However, Saint Martin's Island is a special place because it is the only coral island in Bangladesh.

Question:
Write a short composition on "a tourist place that you visited".

Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:
The name of the tourist place is Saint Martin's Island. -I went there last December. My parents and younger sister were with me. We stayed there for four days. I visited the coral in different shapes and colours. Also I saw very clean and blue water in the Bay of Bengal.
