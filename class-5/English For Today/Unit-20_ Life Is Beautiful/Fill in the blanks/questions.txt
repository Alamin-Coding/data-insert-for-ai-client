Question:
My mother is a homemaker. She gets up early in the morning at (a) _____ and says her Fajr Prayer at (b) _____ . Then she reads the Holy Quran for some time. She starts to prepare our breakfast at (c) _____ and finishes at (d) _____. Then we sit to take our breakfast at (e) _____.

Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:
a) 5:30 am; b) 5:45 am; c) 6:30 am; d) 7:30 am; e) 8:00 am.
