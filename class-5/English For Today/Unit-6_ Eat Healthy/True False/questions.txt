Question:
The bottom level of the Food Pyramid includes foods that provide energy, while the top level contains foods that should be consumed in moderation.

Options:
a. True
b. False


Correct Answer: ক

Solution:


Explanation:
True. The base of the food pyramid contains energy-rich staple foods (like grains, cereals and starchy vegetables) that should form the largest part of the diet, while the top contains fats, oils and sweets that are high in calories but low in nutrients and should be eaten sparingly.

Question:
The bottom level of the Food Pyramid consists of foods high in protein and dairy.

Options:
a. False
b. True


Correct Answer: ক

Solution:


Explanation:
False is correct because the base of the traditional Food Pyramid is made up of carbohydrate-rich foods (grains, bread, cereals) that should be eaten in the largest amounts. Protein and dairy are placed on higher levels and are meant to be eaten in moderate portions, not as the foundation.
