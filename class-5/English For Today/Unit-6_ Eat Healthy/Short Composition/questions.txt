Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:
The food that helps us keep fit is healthy food. Our body needs a good mix of foods from different groups. We should eat right quantity of food. We should eat breads, cereals, rice, noodles, potatoes, etc in plenty. But meat, fish, eggs, nuts and milk should be eaten moderately while fats, oils and sweets should be eaten very little.
