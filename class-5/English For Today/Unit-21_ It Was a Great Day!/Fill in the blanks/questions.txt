Question:
My name is Abid. Last week, I went to the zoo with my parents. We started from home at about (a) _____. It took us one hour to reach there. We entered the zoo at about (b) _____. We stayed there for three hours. We started for home at about (c) _____. We reached home at about (d) _____. At about (e) _____, we took our lunch.

Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:
a) 8:00 a.m; b) 9:00 a.m; c) 12:00 p.m; d) 1:00 p.m; e) 2:00 p.m.
