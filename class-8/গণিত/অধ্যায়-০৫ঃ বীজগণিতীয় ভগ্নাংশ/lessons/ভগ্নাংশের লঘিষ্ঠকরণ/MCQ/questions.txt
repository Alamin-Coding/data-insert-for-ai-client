[math]\frac{x^2+6 x+5}{x^2+10 x+25}[/math] এর সমতুল ভগ্নাংশ হবে-

i. [math]\frac{x+1}{x+5}[/math]

ii. [math]\frac{x^2-2 x-3}{x^2+2 x-15}[/math]

iii. [math]\frac{x^2+2 x+1}{x^2-3 x-10}[/math]

নিচের কোনটি সঠিক?

ক. i ও ii
খ. i ও iii
গ. ii ও iii

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Type: MCQ | Book: গণিত | Years:  | Boards:  | Tags: রাজউক উত্তরা মডেল কলেজ, ঢাকা
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Question:
[math]\frac{6\left(x^2-6 x+5\right)}{8\left(x^2-25\right)}[/math] একটি বীজগাণিতিক ভগ্নাংশ।

ভগ্নাংশটির লব ও হরের গ.সা.গু. কত?

Options:
ক. [math]x - 5[/math]
খ. [math]2(x - 5)[/math]
গ. [math]2(x + 5)[/math]
ঘ. [math]2(x - 1)[/math]


Correct Answer: খ

Solution:
[math]\begin{aligned} &amp; \text { লব }=6\left(\mathrm{x}^2-6 \mathrm{x}+5\right)\\&amp;=3 \times 2\left(\mathrm{x}^2-5 \mathrm{x}-\mathrm{x}+5\right) \\ &amp;=3 \times 2\{\mathrm{x}(\mathrm{x}-5)-1(\mathrm{x}-5)\}\\&amp;=3 \times 2(\mathrm{x}-5) .(\mathrm{x}-1) \\ &amp; \text { হর }=8\left(\mathrm{x}^2-25\right)=2 \times 2 \times 2(\mathrm{x}+5)(\mathrm{x}-5) \\ &amp; \therefore \text { গ.সা.গু. }=2(\mathrm{x}-5)\end{aligned}[/math]
