Question:
[math]\frac{\mathrm{~m}^2}{\mathrm{mn}}, \frac{\mathrm{n}^2}{\mathrm{n} l} \frac{l^2}{l \mathrm{~m}}[/math] কে সমহরবিশিষ্ট ভগ্নাংশে প্রকাশ কর।

Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:
প্রদত্ত ভগ্নাংশগুলো : [math]\frac{\mathrm{m}^2}{\mathrm{mn}}, \frac{\mathrm{n}^2}{\mathrm{n} l}, \frac{l^2}{l \mathrm{~m}}[/math]ভগ্নাংশগুলোর হর [math]\mathrm{mn}, \mathrm{n} l[/math] ও [math]l \mathrm{m}[/math] এর ল.সা. গু [math]=\mathrm{mn} l[math]\therefore \frac{\mathrm{m}^2}{\mathrm{mn}}=\frac{\mathrm{m}^2 \times l}{\mathrm{mn} \times l}=\frac{\mathrm{m}^2 l}{\mathrm{mn} l}[/math]\frac{\mathrm{n}^2}{\mathrm{n} l}=\frac{\mathrm{n}^2 \times \mathrm{m}}{\mathrm{n} l \times \mathrm{m}}=\frac{\mathrm{mn}^2}{\mathrm{mn} l}[/math]এবং [math]\frac{l^2}{l\mathrm{m}}=\frac{l^2 \times \mathrm{m}}{\mathrm{n} l \times \mathrm{m}}=\frac{\mathrm{n} l^2}{\mathrm{mn} l}[/math][math]\therefore[/math] সমহরবিশিষ্ট ভগ্নাংশগুলো : [math]\frac{\mathrm{m}^2 l}{\mathrm{mn} l}, \frac{\mathrm{mn}^2}{\mathrm{mn}l}, \frac{\mathrm{n} l^2}{\mathrm{mn} l}[/math].

Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:

[math]=p^2 q^2[/math]এখন,[math]\frac{p^2+p q}{p^2 q}=\frac{\left(p^2+p q\right) \times q}{p^2 q \times q}\left[\because p^2 q^2 \div p^2 q=q\right][math]=\frac{p^2 q+p q^2}{p^2 q^2}[/math]\begin{aligned}\frac{p^2-p q}{p q^2} &amp; =\frac{\left(p^2-p q\right) \times p}{p q^2 \times p}\left[\because p^2 q^2 \div p q^2=p\right] \\&amp; =\frac{p^3-p^2 q}{p^2 q^2}\end{aligned}[/math][math]\therefore[/math] সাধারণ হরবিশিষ্ট ভগ্নাংশদ্বয় : [math]\frac{p^2 q+p q^2}{p^2 q^2} [/math] ও [math] \frac{p^3-p^2 q}{p^2 q^2}[/math].

Options:


Correct Answer:
