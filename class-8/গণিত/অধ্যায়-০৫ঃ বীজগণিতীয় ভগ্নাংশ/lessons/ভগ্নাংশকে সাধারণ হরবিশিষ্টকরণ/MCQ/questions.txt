Question:
[math]\frac{x+y}{x-y}[/math] ও [math]\frac{x-y}{x+y}[/math] কে সাধারণ হর বিশিষ্ট ভগ্নাংশে প্রকাশ করলে নিচের কোনটি হবে?

Options:
ক. [math]\frac{(x+y)^2}{x^2-y^2}, \frac{(x-y)^2}{x^2-y^2}[/math]
খ. [math]\frac{(x+y)^2}{x-y}, \frac{(x-y)^2}{x+y}[/math]
গ. [math]\frac{(x+y)^2}{x^2+y^2}, \frac{(x-y)^2}{x^2+y^2}[/math]
ঘ. [math]\frac{x-y}{(x+y)^2}, \frac{x+y}{(x-y)^2}[/math]


Correct Answer: ক

Solution:
ভগ্নাংশ দুইটির হর [math](\mathrm{x}-\mathrm{y})[/math] ও [math](\mathrm{x}+\mathrm{y})[/math]এদের ল. সা. গু. [math]=(x+y)(x-y)[/math]১ম ভগ্নাংশ [math]=\frac{x+y}{x-y}=\frac{(x+y) \times(x+y)}{(x-y) \times(x+y)}=\frac{(x+y)^2}{x^2-y^2}[/math]২য় ভগ্নাংশ [math]=\frac{\mathrm{x}-\mathrm{y}}{\mathrm{x}+\mathrm{y}}=\frac{(\mathrm{x}-\mathrm{y}) \times(\mathrm{x}-\mathrm{y})}{(\mathrm{x}+\mathrm{y}) \times(\mathrm{x}-\mathrm{y})}=\frac{(\mathrm{x}-\mathrm{y})^2}{\mathrm{x}^2-\mathrm{y}^2}[/math]
