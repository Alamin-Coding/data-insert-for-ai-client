Question:
P ও Q যথাক্রমে 12 ও 18 এর সকল গুণনীয়কের সেট হলে [math]\mathrm P \cap \mathrm Q[/math] নির্ণয় কর।

Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:
এখানে,[math]\begin{aligned}12 &amp; =1 \times 12 \\&amp; =2 \times 6 \\&amp; =3 \times 4\end{aligned}[math]\therefore \mathrm{P}=\{1,2,3,4,6,12\}[/math]\begin{aligned}\text { আবার, } 18 &amp; =1 \times 18 \\&amp; =2 \times 9 \\&amp; =3 \times 6 \\\therefore \mathrm Q= &amp; \{1,2,3,6,9,18\} \\\therefore \mathrm P \cap \mathrm Q &amp; =\{1,2,3,4,6,12\} \cap\{1,2,3,6,9,18\} \\&amp; =\{1,2,3,6\}\end{aligned}[/math]নির্ণেয় সেট : [math]\mathrm{P} \cap \mathrm{Q}=\{1,2,3,6\}[/math]
