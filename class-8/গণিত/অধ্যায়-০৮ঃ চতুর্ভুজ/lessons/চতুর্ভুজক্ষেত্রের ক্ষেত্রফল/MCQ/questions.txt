Question:
[Image]চিত্রে AB = 6 একক, AD = 5 একক CE = 4 একক, BE = EF AD || BC এবং AD = BC.

△BCF এর ক্ষেত্রফল কত বর্গ একক?

Options:
ক. 12
খ. 20
গ. 24
ঘ. 30


Correct Answer: ক

Solution:
[math]\triangle \mathrm{BCE}[/math] ও [math]\triangle \mathrm{CEF}[/math] এ [math]\mathrm{BE}=\mathrm{EF}, \mathrm{CE}[/math] সাধারণ বাহু এবং [math]\angle \mathrm{CEB}=\angle \mathrm{CEF}=90^{\circ} \therefore \triangle \mathrm{BCE} \cong \triangle \mathrm{CEF}[math] \therefore \mathrm{BC}=\mathrm{CF}[/math]এখন, [math]\triangle \mathrm{BCE}[/math] তে, [math]\mathrm{BE}^2+\mathrm{CE}^2=\mathrm{BC}^2[/math]\begin{aligned}&amp; \mathrm{BE}^2=\mathrm{BC}^2-\mathrm{CE}^2=5^2-4^2=9 \therefore \mathrm{BE}=3 \\&amp; \therefore \mathrm{BF}=2 \mathrm{BE}=3 \times 2 \text { একক }=6 \text { একক } \\&amp; \therefore \triangle \mathrm{BCF}=\frac{1}{2} \times 6 \times 4=12 \text { বর্গ একক }\end{aligned}[/math]
