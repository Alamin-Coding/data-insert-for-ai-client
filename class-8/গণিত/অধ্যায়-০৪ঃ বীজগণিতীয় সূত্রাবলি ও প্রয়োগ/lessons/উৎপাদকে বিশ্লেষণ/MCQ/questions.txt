Question:
[math]2x ^ 2 + 7x - 4[/math] এর উৎপাদকে বিশ্লেষিত রূপ কোনটি?

Options:
ক. (2x - 1)(x - 4)
খ. (2x + 1)(x - 4)
গ. (2x - 1)(x + 4)
ঘ. (2x + 1)(x + 4)&nbsp;


Correct Answer: গ

Solution:
[math]\begin{aligned} &amp; 2 x^2+7 x-4 \\ &amp; =2 x^2+8 x-x-4 \\ &amp; =2 x(x+4)-1(x+4) \\ &amp; =(x+4)(2 x-1)\end{aligned}[/math]
