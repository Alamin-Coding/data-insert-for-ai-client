Question:
উৎপাদকে বিশ্লেষণ কর: [math]a ^ 4 + a ^ 2  b ^ 2 + b ^ 4[/math].

Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:
[math]\begin{aligned} &amp; a^4+a^2 b^2+b^4 \\ &amp; =\left(a^2\right)^2+2 \cdot a^2 \cdot b^2+\left(b^2\right)^2-a^2 b^2 \\ &amp; =\left(a^2+b^2\right)-(a b)^2 \\ &amp; =\left(a^2+b^2+a b\right)\left(a^2+b^2-a b\right) \\ &amp; =\left(a^2+a b+b^2\right)\left(a^2-a b+b^2\right)\end{aligned}[/math]
