The teacher advises the students to __________ English with their classmates.

Options:
a. ignore
b. speak
c. study
d. write


Correct Answer: খ

Solution:
Solution:
Step 1: Recognize the structure "advise the students to + base verb", so we need a base-form verb that makes sense in context.  
Step 2: Meaning — when a teacher tells students to use the language with classmates, the natural action is to practice speaking; the common collocation is "speak English with their classmates."  
Step 3: Eliminate other options: "ignore" contradicts a teacher's advice, "write" is less likely as a spoken classroom activity, and while "study" is possible, "speak" more directly fits the idea of using/practicing the language in class.  
Therefore the correct answer is b: speak.

How does one of the students ask for a pen?

Options:


Correct Answer: গ

Solution:
Solution:
Step 1: Identify the intent — the student wants permission to use someone else's pen temporarily, so the correct speech act is a polite request to borrow.
