Options:

Correct Answer: খ

Solution:
Solution:
Step 1: Identify common characteristics of Generation Z—born into widespread internet and smartphone use, they are "digital natives" who adopt and use technology fluently.  
Step 2: Compare each option to that trait: option (b) "Highly digitally savvy" matches directly; (a) "Less connected than previous generations" contradicts the evidence; (c) "Disinterested in global issues" is false as many in Gen Z are actively engaged with social and global topics; (d) "Isolation due to social media" can be a possible consequence for some individuals but is not a defining characteristic.
