Drought has led to __________ in agricultural output in Bangladesh.

Options:
a. Increased yield
b. Loss
c. No change
d. Stability


Correct Answer: b

Solution:
Solution:
Step 1: Drought means prolonged shortage of water, which reduces soil moisture and limits irrigation water supply.  
Step 2: Reduced water availability stresses crops, lowers growth and yields, and can cause partial or complete crop failure.  
Step 3: Therefore the direct effect on agricultural output is a loss (reduction) in production. Hence the correct answer is (b) Loss.

Brief note on other options: (a) Increased yield is inconsistent with reduced water; (c) No change and (d) Stability contradict observed negative impacts of drought on crop production.
