Question:
Change the following sentences as directed in the brackets.

(a) Mr. Ant made proper use of his time. (Negative)

(b) Mr. Grasshopper used to pass his time in singing only. (Make it negative)

(c) He was a very worthless person. (Make it exclamatory)

(d) He could not but suffer at last. (Make it affirmative)

(e) We should learn a lesson from this story. (Imperative)

Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:
(a) Mr. Ant did not waste his time.

(b) Mr. Grasshopper did not do anything but sing.

(c) What a worthless person he was!

(d) He had to suffer at last.

(e) Learn a lesson from this story.

Explanation:
(a) Negative: “made proper use” → “did not waste”।

(b) Negative রূপ: “did not do anything but sing”।

(c) Exclamatory: “What a worthless person…” কাঠামো ব্যবহার।

(d) Affirmative: “could not but” → “had to”।

(e) Imperative: “should” বাদ দিয়ে মূল verb দিয়ে শুরু।
