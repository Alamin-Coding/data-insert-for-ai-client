--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Question:
[math]\frac{x^{2}+2 x-15}{x^{2}+9 x+20}[/math] একটি ভগ্নাংশ। এর সমতুল ভগ্নাংশ কোনটি?

Options:
ক. [math]\frac{x+5}{x+4}[/math]
খ. [math]\frac{x+5}{x-3}[/math]
গ. [math]\frac{x-3}{x-4}[/math]
ঘ. [math]\frac{x-3}{x+4}[/math]


Correct Answer: ঘ

Solution:
[math]\frac{x^{2}+2 x-15}{x^{2}+9 x+20}=\frac{x^{2}+5 x-3 x-15}{x^{2}+5 x+4 x+20}=\frac{x(x+5)-3(x+5)}{x(x+5)+4(x+5)}[/math]
= [math]\frac{(x+5)(x-3)}{(x+5)(x+4)}=\frac{x-3}{x+4}[/math]

Options:
ক. [math]\frac{x+3}{x+1}[/math]
খ. [math]\frac{x+3}{x-4}[/math]
গ. [math]\frac{x+5}{x-7}[/math]
ঘ. [math]\frac{x-5}{x-6}[/math]


Correct Answer: A.[math]\frac{x+3}{x+1}[/math]

Solution:
[math]\frac{x^{2}+5 x+6}{x^{2}+3 x+2}[/math]
= [math]\frac{x^{2}+2 x+3 x+6}{x^{2}+2 x+x+2}[/math]
= [math]\frac{x(x+2)+3(x+2)}{x(x+2)+1(x+2)}[/math]
 = [math]\frac{(x+2)(x+3)}{(x+2)(x+1)}=\frac{x+3}{x+1}[/math]
