Question:
[math](2x^2 + x)(3x - 5) =[/math] কত?

Options:
A. [math]6x^3 + 7x^2 - 5x[/math]
B. [math]6x^3 - 7x^2 - 5x[/math]
C. [math]6x^2 - 5x[/math]
D. [math]6x^3 + x - 5[/math]


Correct Answer: খ

Solution:
[math]2x^2 \cdot 3x = 6x^3[/math]
, [math]2x^2 \cdot -5 = -10x^2[/math]
, [math]x \cdot 3x = 3x^2[/math]
, [math]x \cdot -5 = -5x[/math]
 → যোগফল: [math]6x^3 - 7x^2 - 5x[/math]
।
