Question:
In a chart, if 20 students prefer apples and 30 prefer bananas, what is the total number of students surveyed?

Options:
a. 50 students
b. 20 students
c. 30 students
d. 70 students


Correct Answer: a

Solution:
Solution:
Step 1: Total students = (students who prefer apples) + (students who prefer bananas).  
Step 2: Calculate [math]Total = 20 + 30 = 50[/math].  
Therefore the correct answer is option a: 50 students.  
Briefly, options b and c list only one category (20 or 30) so they are incomplete, and option d (70) is an incorrect sum.
