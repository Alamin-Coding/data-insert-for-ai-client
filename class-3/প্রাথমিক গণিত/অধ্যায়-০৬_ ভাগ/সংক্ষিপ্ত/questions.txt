Question:
How does the division of items among friends demonstrate the concept of equal distribution in mathematical terms?

Options:


Correct Answer: It illustrates how to use division to fairly allocate resources among a set number of people.

Solution:
Solution:
Answer approach — Equal distribution means dividing a total number of identical items into the same-sized parts so each person gets an equal share. Mathematically this is performed by division: compute the total divided by the number of people.

Essential steps (algorithmic):
1. Count the total items [math]T[/math] and the number of people [math]n[/math].
2. Compute the quotient and remainder by division: [math]T \div n[/math] or [math]\frac{T}{n}[/math].
3. Interpret the result:
   - If [math]n[/math] divides [math]T[/math] exactly, each person gets the integer share [math]\frac{T}{n}[/math].
   - If not, each person gets a fractional share [math]\frac{T}{n}=q+\frac{r}{n}[/math], where [math]q[/math] is the integer quotient and [math]r[/math] the remainder ([math]0<r<n[/math]).

Relevant concepts and principles:
- Division as partitioning: division partitions a whole into equal parts.
- Division is the inverse of multiplication: if each person gets a share [math]s[/math], then [math]n\times s = T[/math].
- If items are indivisible units and a remainder exists, you can either convert units to smaller divisible parts (fractions) or distribute the remainder by an agreed rule (e.g., some get one extra).
- Fairness requires identical shares for all people.

Logical reasoning example (adds value):
- Example 1 (evenly divisible): 12 cookies among 4 friends: [math]12\div4=3[/math]. So each friend gets 3 cookies because [math]4\times3=12[/math].
- Example 2 (remainder): 10 candies among 4 friends: [math]10\div4=2\text{ remainder }2[/math]. As equal fractional shares, each gets [math]\frac{10}{4}=2+\frac{2}{4}=2.5[/math] candies; if candies must be whole, two candies remain to be allocated by a rule.

Conclusion:
Division formalizes equal distribution: compute [math]\frac{T}{n}[/math] to determine each person's equal share, interpreting the quotient and remainder according to whether fractional division is allowed.

Explanation:
ব্যাখ্যা: এটি দেখায় যে একই পরিমাণ প্রতিজনকে দিতে ভাগ (division) ব্যবহার করা হয় — মোট আইটেমকে মানুষের সংখ্যায় ভাগ করে প্রতিজনের জন্য সমান অংশ নির্ণয় করা যায়। উদাহরণস্বরূপ, 12টি আইটেম 4 জনে সমানভাবে ভাগ করলে [math]12 ÷ 4 = 3[/math], অর্থাৎ প্রতিজন 3টি পায়, যা ন্যায্য বন্টন দেখায়।
