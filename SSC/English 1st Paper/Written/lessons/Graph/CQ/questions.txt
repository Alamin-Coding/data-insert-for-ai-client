Question:
Look at the following pie-chart. It represents the passing rates of different subjects of the students of a school. Now, describe the chart in your own language. You should highlight and summarise the information given in the chart.

[math]~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~[/math] Passing rates of different subjects


[Image]


Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:
The given pie-chart illustrates the passing rates of four different subjects - Bangla, English, Mathematics and Science. According to the chart, it is found that the passing rate is in a pleasant level in Bangla. It is 88%, the highest among all the subjects. The second highest rate is in English and it is 85%. The passing rate of Mathematics is 80%. It is in the third place of the mentioned subjects. But, the lowest passing rate is found in Science. It is 75%. On the whole the students are doing better in Bangla. However, the passing rate in Science is unsatisfactory.

Question:
The graph below shows the choice of profession by different educated people. Describe the graph in your own words. You should highlight and summarize the information given in the graph.


[Image]


Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:
The column graph shows the choice of profession by different educated people. The graph represents the typical trend among the educated people who are the enlightened representatives of our society. The graph shows that the highest portion of educated people choose govt. job as their profession or career. In percentage, 53 persons out of 100 have shown their preference for govt. job. Next comes business which is the choice of 26% educated person as their profession. It means, every one person out of four choose business as career. The third highest and the second lowest portion of people have shown their likeness for farming as their profession, In an agricultural country like ours, it is not hopeful that only 12 percent of the educated people choose farming as their profession. The rest and the lowest portion of the educated people have been found to choose different types of intellectual works like writing journalism, performing in various branches percent educated people have shown their likeness for intellectual works as profession or career. If we analyse the data minutely, we will easily find that the socio-economic and cultural set-up of our country has a great influence on the educated people in choosing profession. If the social security net of our country would be stronger, the trend of choosing profession would have also been more balanced. The govt. should give importance on this issue.

Word Meaning-(শব্দার্থ): represent-প্রকাশ করা। preference-পছন্দ। intellectual- প্রতিনিধি, enlightened -আলোকিত। representatives-বুদ্ধিবৃত্তিক। journalism-সাংবাদিকতা। analyse-বিশ্লেষণ করা। minutely – 'সঠিকভাবে।-influence প্রভাব।

Explanation:
Explanation: The graph compares how educated people are distributed across different professions by showing the height of bars (or points) for each job category on the vertical axis and the professions on the horizontal axis. The correct summary picks the profession with the tallest bar/point as the most popular and the one with the shortest as the least popular, because bar/point heights represent numbers or percentages. Thus, the best description simply states which profession has the highest share, which has the lowest, and notes any clear middle-range or balanced choices.
