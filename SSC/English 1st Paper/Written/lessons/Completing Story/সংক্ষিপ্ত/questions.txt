Question:

Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:


Explanation:
Because repeated defeats undermine confidence and the likelihood of success, being beaten six times made Robert Bruce lose hope of overcoming King Edward I. Repeated military failures demoralize leaders and troops, so losing hope after many losses is a natural consequence.

Question:
Once there was a clever fox in a jungle. One day, when the fox was walking through the jongle, he fell into a trap. he could somehow get out of the trap, but he had to leave ………….

Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:


Explanation:
The correct answer is "his tail" because the trap had caught the fox by its tail, so to pull himself out he had to leave the tail behind. This shows the idea that when danger threatens your life, you may have to give up something less important to escape safely.

Question:
One day, a crow became very thirsty. He flew from place to place in search of water. But he found no water. He continued his search for ………..

Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:


Explanation:
Correct answer: "a long time." Because he did not find water, he kept looking for a prolonged period; grammatically "continued his search for" needs a duration (e.g., "a long time" or "some time"), so "a long time" best fits the sentence.
