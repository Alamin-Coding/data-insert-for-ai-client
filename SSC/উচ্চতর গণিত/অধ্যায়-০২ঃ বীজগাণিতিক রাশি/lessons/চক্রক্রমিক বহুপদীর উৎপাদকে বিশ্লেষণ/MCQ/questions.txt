ক. -8
খ. -2
গ. 1

Correct Answer: খ

Solution:

বা, $a+c=2 b$

Question:
(x−y)3+(y−z)3+(z−x)3(x−y)3+(y−z)3+(z−x)3(x-y)^3+(y-z)^3+(z-x)^3 এর উৎপাদক বিশ্লেষণ কোনটি?

Options:
ক. $(x-y)(y-z)(z-x)$
খ. $(x+y)(y+z)(z+x)$
গ. $3(x-y)(y-z)(z-x)$
ঘ. $3(x+y)(y+z)(z+x)$


Correct Answer: গ

Solution:
প্রদত্ত রাশি, $(x-y)^3+(y-z)^3+(z-x)^3$
$\begin{array}{r}\text { ধরি, } A=x-y \\ B=y-z \\ C=z-x\end{array}$
$\begin{aligned} &amp; \therefore \quad \mathrm{A}+\mathrm{B}+\mathrm{C}=\mathrm{x}-\mathrm{y}+\mathrm{y}-\mathrm{z}+\mathrm{z}-\mathrm{x}=\mathbf{0} \\ &amp; \therefore \mathrm{A}^3+\mathrm{B}^3+\mathrm{C}^3=3 \mathrm{ABC}\end{aligned}$
$\therefore(x-y)^3+(y-z)^3+(z-x)^3\\=3(x-y)(y-z)(z-x)$.

$\therefore \mathrm{a}^3+\mathrm{b}^3+\mathrm{c}^3=3 \mathrm{abc}$.
