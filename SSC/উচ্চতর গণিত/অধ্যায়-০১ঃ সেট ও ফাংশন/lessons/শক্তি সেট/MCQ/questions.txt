Question:
A={1}A={1}A=\{1\} এবং B={3}B={3}B=\{3\} হলে, P(A∩B)=P(A∩B)=P(A \cap B)= কত ?

Options:
ক. $\{\varnothing\}$
খ. $\{\varnothing,\{1\}\}$
গ. $\{\varnothing,\{3\}\}$
ঘ. $\{\varnothing,\{1\},\{3\},\{1,3\}$


Correct Answer: ক

Solution:
$A=\{1\}$ এবং $B=\{3\}$
$
\begin{aligned}

\end{aligned}
$
