Solution:

$

বা, $(x+4)(x+5)=0$
$
\therefore x=-4,-5
$
যেহেতু $x \in \mathbb{N}$ সেহেতু $A=\{ \}$
$
\therefore \mathrm{n}(\mathrm{~A})=0 \text {. }

Question:
A∩B=ϕ,n(A)=3A∩B=ϕ,n(A)=3A \cap B=\phi, n(A)=3 এবং n(A∪B)=10n(A∪B)=10n(A \cup B)=10 হলে n(B)=n(B)=n(B)= ?

Options:
ক. 13
খ. 10
গ. 7
ঘ. 3


Correct Answer: গ

Solution:
$\begin{aligned} &amp;  \text { দেওয়া আছে, } A \cap B=\varnothing, n(A)=3 \\ &amp; \quad \text { এবং } n(A \cup B)=10 \\ &amp; \therefore n(A \cap B)=0 \\ &amp; \text { এখন, } n(A \cup B)=n(A)+n(B)-n(A \cap B) \\ &amp; \text { বা, } 10=3+n(B)-0 \\ &amp; \text { বা, } n(B)=10-3 \\ &amp; \therefore n(B)=7 .\end{aligned}$
