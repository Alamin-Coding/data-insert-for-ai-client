Question:
[Image]△ ABC এর ভরকেন্দ্র G হলে নিচের কোনটি সঠিক?

Options:
ক. $\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}-\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AC}}=\overrightarrow{\mathrm{BC}}$
খ. $\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AC}}=\overrightarrow{\mathrm{BC}}$
গ. $\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AC}}=2 \overrightarrow{\mathrm{AD}}$
ঘ. $\overrightarrow{A D}+\overrightarrow{B E}-\overrightarrow{C F}=0$


Correct Answer: গ

Solution:
[Image]এখানে, ∆ABC এর G ভরকেন্দ্র
$\triangle A B D \text{-এ,} \overrightarrow{A B}+\overrightarrow{B D}=\overrightarrow{A D}$
বা, $\overrightarrow{A B}=\overrightarrow{A D}-\overrightarrow{B D}$ $.........(i)$
$\triangle \mathrm{ADC} \text{-এ,} \overrightarrow{\mathrm{AD}}+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{DC}}=\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AC}}$
$
\therefore \quad \overrightarrow{\mathrm{AC}}=\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AD}}+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{DC}}............(ii)
$
(i) ও (ii)নং যোগ করে পাই,
$
\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AC}}=\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AD}}-\overrightarrow{\mathrm{BD}}+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AD}}+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{DC}}
$
$
\begin{aligned}
&amp; =2 \overrightarrow{A D}-\overrightarrow{B D}+\overrightarrow{B D}[\because \overrightarrow{B D}=\overrightarrow{D C}] \\
&amp; \therefore \overrightarrow{A B}+\overrightarrow{A C}=2 \overrightarrow{A D}
\end{aligned}
$

Question:
[Image]যদি △ PQR-এ PQ ও PR এর মধ্যবিন্দু যথাক্রমে M ও N হয়, তবে নিচের কোনটি সঠিক?

Options:
ক. $\overrightarrow{\mathrm{QR}}=2(\overrightarrow{\mathrm{PN}}-\overrightarrow{\mathrm{PM}})$
খ. $\overrightarrow{\mathrm{QR}}=2(\overrightarrow{\mathrm{PM}}-\overrightarrow{\mathrm{PN}})$
গ. $\overrightarrow{\mathrm{QR}}=2(\overrightarrow{\mathrm{PN}}+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{PM}})$
ঘ. $\overrightarrow{\mathrm{QR}}=2(\overrightarrow{\mathrm{PQ}}+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{PR}})$


Correct Answer: ক

Solution:
[Image]M, PQ এর মধ্যবিন্দু এবং N, PR এর মধ্যবিন্দু
$
\therefore \overrightarrow{\mathrm{PM}}=\frac{1}{2} \overrightarrow{\mathrm{PQ}}
$
বা, $\overrightarrow{\mathrm{PQ}}=2 \overrightarrow{\mathrm{PM}} \quad \therefore \overrightarrow{\mathrm{PN}}=\frac{1}{2} \overrightarrow{\mathrm{PR}}$
বা, $\overrightarrow{\mathrm{PR}}=2 \overrightarrow{\mathrm{PN}}$
এখন, $\triangle \mathrm{PQR}\text{-এ,} \overrightarrow{\mathrm{PQ}}+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{QR}}=\overrightarrow{\mathrm{PR}}$
বা, $\overrightarrow{\mathrm{QR}}=\overrightarrow{\mathrm{PR}}-\overrightarrow{\mathrm{PQ}}=2 \overrightarrow{\mathrm{PN}}-2 \overrightarrow{\mathrm{PM}}=2(\overrightarrow{\mathrm{PN}}-\overrightarrow{\mathrm{PM}})$.

Question:

ক. $\frac{1}{2}\overrightarrow{\mathrm{BC}}$
খ. $\frac{1}{2} \overrightarrow{\mathrm{AC}}$
গ. $\frac{1}{2} \overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}$
ঘ. $\frac{1}{2} \overrightarrow{\mathrm{BE}}$

Solution:

Question:
[Image]ABCD সামান্তরিকের  −−→ABAB→\overrightarrow{\mathbf{A B}} কে −−→ACAC→\overrightarrow{\mathbf{A C}} ও −−→BDBD→\overrightarrow{\mathbf{B D}} এর মাধ্যমে প্রকাশ করলে নিচের কোনটি সঠিক?

Options:
ক. $\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}=\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AC}}+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{BD}}$
খ. $\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}=\frac{1}{2}(\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AC}}+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{BD}})$
গ. $\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}=\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AC}}-\overrightarrow{\mathrm{BD}}$
ঘ. $\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}=\frac{1}{2}(\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AC}}-\overrightarrow{\mathrm{BD}})$


Correct Answer: ঘ

Solution:
\(\Delta\)ABO এ ভেক্টর যোগের ত্রিভুজ বিধি অনুসারে,
$\begin{aligned} &amp; \overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{BO}}=\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AO}} \\ &amp; \text { বা, } \overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}=\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AO}}-\overrightarrow{\mathrm{BO}} \\ &amp; \text { বা, } \overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}=\frac{1}{2} \overrightarrow{\mathrm{AC}}-\frac{1}{2} \overrightarrow{\mathrm{BD}} \\ &amp; \therefore \overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}=\frac{1}{2}(\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AC}}-\overrightarrow{\mathrm{BD}}).\end{aligned}$
