খ. $S=\{x \in R: x \geq-6\}$

Options:
ক. <span class="math-tex">\((3,-2)\)</span>
খ. <span class="math-tex">\((-3,8)\)</span>
গ. <span class="math-tex">\((-3,-5)\)</span>

Solution:

$\therefore(-3,8)$ বিন্দুতে $3 x+2 y&gt;5$ অসমতাটি সিদ্ধ হয়।

Options:
ক. <span class="math-tex">\((-3,3)\)</span>
খ. <span class="math-tex">\((2,5)\)</span>
গ. <span class="math-tex">\((0,1)\)</span>
ঘ. <span class="math-tex">\((2,-1)\)</span>

Solution:

$(2,5)$ হলে, $2.2+3.5-3=4+15-3=16&gt;0$
$(0,1)$ হলে, $0+3.1-3=0$
$(2,-1)$ হলে, $2.2+3(-1)-3=4-3-3=-2&lt;0$.
