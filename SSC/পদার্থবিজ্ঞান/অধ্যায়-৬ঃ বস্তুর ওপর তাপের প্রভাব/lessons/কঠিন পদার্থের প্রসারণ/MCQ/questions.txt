Question:
নিচের কোনটি সঠিক?

Options:
ক. $\mathrm{L}_2=\mathrm{L}_1+\alpha \mathrm{I}_2\left(\mathrm{~T}_2-\mathrm{T}_1\right)$
খ. $\mathrm{A}_2=\mathrm{A}_1+\beta \mathrm{A}_1\left(\mathrm{~T}_1-\mathrm{T}_2\right)$
গ. $\mathrm{V}_2=\mathrm{V}_1+\gamma \mathrm{V}_1\left(\mathrm{~T}_2-\mathrm{T}_1\right)$
ঘ. $\alpha=2 \beta=3 \gamma$


Correct Answer: গ

Solution:
$\Delta \mathrm{V} \propto \mathrm{V}_1$ এবং $\Delta \mathrm{V} \propto \Delta \mathrm{T}$
$\therefore \mathrm{V} \propto \mathrm{V}_1 \Delta \mathrm{~T}$ বা, $\Delta \mathrm{V}=\gamma \mathrm{V}_1 \Delta \mathrm{~T}$
বা, $\mathrm{V}_2-\mathrm{V}_1=\gamma \mathrm{V}_1\left(\mathrm{~T}_2-\mathrm{T}_1\right)$
$ \therefore \mathrm{V}_2=\mathrm{V}_1+\gamma \mathrm{V}_1\left(\mathrm{~T}_2-\mathrm{T}_1\right)$

Options:
ক. $19.30 \mathrm{gm} / \mathrm{cc}$
খ. $19.28 \mathrm{gm} / \mathrm{cc}$
গ. $19.22 \mathrm{gm} / \mathrm{cc}$

$
\gamma=3 \alpha=3 \times 14 \times 10^{-6}{ }^{\circ} \mathrm{C}^{-1}=42 \times 10^{-6}{ }^{\circ} \mathrm{C}^{-1}
$

$
\begin{aligned}
V &amp; =V_0(1+\gamma \Delta \theta) \\
&amp; =1\left(1+42 \times 10^{-6} \times 100\right) \mathrm{cm}^3  \\
&amp; =1.0042 \mathrm{~cm}^3
\end{aligned}
$
$\therefore$ পরিবর্তিত ঘনত্ব হবে $=\frac{19.3}{1.0042}=19.22 \mathrm{gm} / \mathrm{cc}$
