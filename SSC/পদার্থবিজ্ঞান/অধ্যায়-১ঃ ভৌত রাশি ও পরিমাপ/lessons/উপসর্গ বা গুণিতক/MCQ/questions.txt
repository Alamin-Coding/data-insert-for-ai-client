Question:
1 Pa = কত atm?

Options:
ক. 76
খ. 10<sup>5</sup>
গ. $9.8723 \times 10^{-3}$
ঘ. $9.8723 \times 10^{-6}$


Correct Answer: ঘ

Solution:
আমরা জানি, $101325 \mathrm{~Pa}=1 \mathrm{~atm}$
$$
\begin{aligned}
\therefore \quad 1 \mathrm{~Pa} &amp; =\frac{1}{101325} \mathrm{~atm}=9.8692 \times 10^{-6} \mathrm{~atm} \\
&amp; \approx 9.8723 \times 10^{-6} \cdot \mathrm{~atm}
\end{aligned}
$$
