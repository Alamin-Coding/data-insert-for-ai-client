Question:
1 MJ, 1 nJ এর কত গুণ?

Options:
ক. $10^{-9}$
খ. $10^{9}$
গ. $10^{12}$
ঘ. $10^{15}$


Correct Answer: ঘ

Solution:
$\frac{1 \mathrm{MJ}}{1 \mathrm{~nJ}}=\frac{1 \times 10^6 \mathrm{~J}}{1 \times 10^{-9} \mathrm{~J}}=1 \times 10^{15}$
