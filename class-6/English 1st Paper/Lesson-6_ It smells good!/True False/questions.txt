Question:
Both the celebration of national days in Bangladesh and Suraiya Begum's visits to elderly people demonstrate the importance of community engagement and honoring sacrifices in society.

Options:
a. True
b. False


Correct Answer: a

Solution:


Explanation:
ব্যাখ্যা: উভয় কৃত্যই সমাজে নৈতিক দায়িত্ববোধ এবং ত্যাগের মূল্যকে স্মরণ ও সম্মান জানায় — জাতীয় দিবস পালনে ইতিহাস শ্রদ্ধা এবং সৌরায়া বেগমের বৃদ্ধদের সাক্ষাত স্বেচ্ছাসেবী সম্পর্ক ও সহানুভূতির মাধ্যমে কমিউনিটি এনগেজমেন্টকে প্রকাশ করে। তাই বিবৃতিটি সত্য।

Question:
Suraiya Begum's plan to establish an old people's home is motivated by her own feelings of boredom and a desire to help the elderly, similar to how the Bangladeshi people celebrate their Victory Day to honor sacrifices made for independence.

Options:
a. True
b. False


Correct Answer: a

Solution:


Explanation:
Both actions are driven by emotional motives: Suraiya wants to relieve her boredom and serve elderly people by creating a home for them, while Bangladeshis celebrate Victory Day to honor and remember the sacrifices made for independence. Because both involve acting out of respect, care, or remembrance for others, the statement is True.
