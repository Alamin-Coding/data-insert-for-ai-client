Honey is ineffective for treating cough.

Options:
a. True
b. False


Correct Answer: b

Solution:
Solution:
Step 1: Clinical evidence (randomized trials and systematic reviews) shows that honey reduces cough frequency and severity and improves sleep compared with placebo or no treatment, so honey can be effective for symptomatic relief of acute cough.
