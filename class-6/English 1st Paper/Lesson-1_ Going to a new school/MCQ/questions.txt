Question:
How does Bulbul's daily routine highlight the importance of his job in the community, and what could be the potential consequences if he were to fall sick again?

Options:
a. His routine is not important; if he falls sick, the area remains clean.
b. His job is crucial for cleanliness; if he is sick, rubbish will accumulate, causing problems.
c. He collects rubbish only occasionally; sickness has no effect on the community.
d. The people appreciate him, but his absence causes no significant issues.


Correct Answer: খ

Solution:
Solution:
Bulbul’s daily routine of collecting rubbish every day shows that his work is essential for keeping the neighborhood clean and preventing waste buildup. If he were to fall sick again, rubbish would start to accumulate, leading to bad smells, health hazards, and environmental problems—so the community would be directly affected. Therefore option b is correct.

Explanation why others are wrong:
- Option a is false because the routine clearly maintains cleanliness; absence would not leave the area clean.  
- Option c is wrong because the text indicates regular (daily) collection, not occasional work.  
- Option d is incorrect because people’s appreciation does not negate the practical negative effects of his absence.
