Question:
Both Suraiya Begum and Hason Raja are involved in helping elderly people, but while Suraiya plans to establish an old people's home, Hason Raja focused on expressing his thoughts through poetry and songs.

Options:
a. True
b. False


Correct Answer: a

Solution:


Explanation:
True — Suraiya Begum plans to establish an old people's home, a direct form of social care, while Hason Raja expressed his feelings and concerns about life and society through poetry and songs. The key point is that both are concerned with older people and society, but Suraiya's approach is practical welfare and Hason Raja's is artistic expression.

a. True
b. False


Correct Answer: a

Solution:


Explanation:

Similarly, Mariam’s visits with her aunt to provide companionship are acts of respect and care; both describe communal, commemorative behaviors that honor and support others.
