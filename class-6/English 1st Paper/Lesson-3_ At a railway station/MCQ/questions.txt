Question:
Birishiri is near the Arabian Sea.

Options:
a. True
b. False


Correct Answer: b

Solution:
Solution: Birishiri is a village in Netrokona district in the Mymensingh division of Bangladesh, located inland in the haor (wetland) region. Since Birishiri is inland in northeastern/central Bangladesh and not near either coast, the statement "Birishiri is near the Arabian Sea" is False.
