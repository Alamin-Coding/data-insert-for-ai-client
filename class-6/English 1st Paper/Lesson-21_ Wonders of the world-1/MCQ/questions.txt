Why the others are less appropriate: option b ("blade, break") has the [math]/eɪ/[/math] diphthong (so it is a diphthong pair but not the one targeted here), option d ("ball, bird") contains r-colored or monophthongal vowels, and option a repeats "close," which is less useful as a distinct pair.
