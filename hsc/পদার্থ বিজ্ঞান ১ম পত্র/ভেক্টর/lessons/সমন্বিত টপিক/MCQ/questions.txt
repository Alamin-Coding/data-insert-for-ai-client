উদ্দীপকের আলোকে কোনটি সঠিক?

Options:
A. [math]\vec{L}+\vec{N}-\vec{M}=0[/math]
B. [math]\mathrm{L}+\vec{\mathrm{M}}+\vec{\mathrm{N}}=0[/math]
C. [math]\vec{\mathrm{L}}+\vec{\mathrm{M}}-\vec{\mathrm{N}}=0[/math]
D. [math]\vec{M}+\vec{N}-L=0[/math]


Correct Answer: ঘ

Solution:
[math]\overrightarrow N + \overrightarrow M = \overrightarrow L\\\therefore \overrightarrow M + \overrightarrow N - \overrightarrow L = 0[/math]
