Question:
One afternoon, Jamil and his friends were gossiping beside a lake. All on a sudden, Jamil noticed that a bubble from the bottom of the lake of transparent water was coming out on the surface of the water. After coming to the surface, the bubble took a large size. Size of the bubble on the surface was 5 times, and atmospheric pressure was [math]10^{5} \mathrm{Nm}^{-2} .[/math] [Density of water, [math]p = 1000 kgm³[/math]]. Calculate the depth of the lake.

Options:
A. [math]38.82 m[/math]
B. [math]40.82 m[/math]
C. [math]42.82 m[/math]
D. [math]44.82 m[/math]


Correct Answer: B.[math]40.82 m[/math]

Solution:
[math]\frac{P_{a}+P_{w}}{P_{a}}=5 \Rightarrow P_{a}+P_{w}=5 P_{a} \Rightarrow P_{w}=4 P_{a}[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow h \rho g=4 \mathrm{P}_{\mathrm{a}} \Rightarrow \mathrm{h}=\frac{4 \mathrm{P}_{\mathrm{a}}}{\rho \mathrm{g}}=\frac{4 \times 10^{5}}{10^{3} \times 9.8} \mathrm{~m}=40.82 \mathrm{~m}[/math]

Question:
A bubble rises from the bottom of a lake of depth [math]80.0\ m[/math] where the temperature is [math]4^{\circ} \mathrm{C}[/math]. The water temperature at the surface is [math]18^{\circ} \mathrm{C}[/math]. If the bubble's initial diameter is [math]1.0 \mathrm{~mm}[/math], what is diameter when it reaches the surface?

Options:
A. 2.35mm
B. 2.10mm
C. 2.50mm
D. 2.40mm


Correct Answer: খ

Solution:
[math]\dfrac{P_{1} V_{1}}{T_{1}}=\dfrac{P_{2} V_{2}}{T_{2}} \Rightarrow \dfrac{P_{1} d_{1}^{3}}{T_{1}}=\dfrac{P_{2} d_{2}^{3}}{T_{2}}\\ P_{1}=101.325 \times 10^{3}+80 \times 1000 \times 9.8 ;\\ P_{2}=101.325 \times 10^{3} \therefore d_{2}=2.10 \mathrm{~mm}[/math]

 [math]\Rightarrow \mathrm{h}=\frac{5 \mathrm{P}_{2}-\mathrm{P}_{2}}{\rho \mathrm{g}}=\frac{4 \mathrm{P}_{2}}{\rho \mathrm{g}}=\frac{4 \times 10^{5}}{1000 \times 9.8}=40.81 \mathrm{~m}[/math]
