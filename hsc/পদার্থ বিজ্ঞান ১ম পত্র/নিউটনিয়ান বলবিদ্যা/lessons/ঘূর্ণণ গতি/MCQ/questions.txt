ঘ. 98.7

[math]\text { Hints: } \mathrm{T}=\frac{\mathrm{mv} \mathrm{r}^{2}}{\mathrm{r}}=\mathrm{m} \omega^{2} \mathrm{r}[/math]
 [math]\\[/math]
