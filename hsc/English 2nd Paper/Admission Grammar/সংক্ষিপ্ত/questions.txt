Question:
The word 'productivity' is a/an -

Options:


Correct Answer: ঘ

Solution:
কোনো word এর শেষে →ity, tion, th, er, ief → থাকলে noun হয়। যেমন - ability, creation, honesty, writer, belief।

Explanation:
Explanation: "Productivity" is a noun because it names a concept or quality — the rate or amount of output produced. It is formed from the adjective "productive" plus the suffix "-ity," which turns adjectives into nouns.
