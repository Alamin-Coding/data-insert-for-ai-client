Question:
'I was there __that time' we need-

Options:


Correct Answer: C.

Solution:


Explanation:
'at' is used with specific points or moments in time, so "I was there at that time" is correct. 'In' fits longer periods (e.g., "in the morning") and 'on' fits days or dates (e.g., "on Monday").
