Question:
The correct form of the possessive has been maintained in ----.

Options:
A. Charles friend's loves Burns Poems
B. Charles friend loves Burns Poems
C. Charles friend loves Burns Poems
D. Charles's friend loves Burns's Poem.


Correct Answer: ঘ

Solution:
Possessive সবসময় functional noun-এর পূর্বে বসে। এখানে বলা হচ্ছে: চার্লসের বন্ধু বার্নসের কবিতা ভালোবাসে। Friend এবং Poem হচ্ছে এখানে functional noun।

Choose the correct sentence He gave ------.

Options:
A. to the class a tough assignment
B. the class a tough assignment
C. a tough assignment for the class
D. an assignment very tough to the class

Correct Answer: খ

Solution:
