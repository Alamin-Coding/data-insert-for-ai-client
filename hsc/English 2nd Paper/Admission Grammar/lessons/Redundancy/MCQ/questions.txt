Question:
Which is the correct sentence?

Options:
A. This is a true
B. This is true fact
C. This is fact
D. This is a fact


Correct Answer: ঘ

Solution:
This is a fact

Explanation:
redundancy হলো কোনো বাক্যে একই অর্থ সম্পন্ন একাধিক শব্দের পুনরাবৃত্তি । উল্লিখিত প্রশ্নে true এবং fact প্রায় একই অর্থ প্রকাশ করে । তাই option (ঘ) সঠিক উত্তর ।

Question:
Which is the correct sentence?

Options:
a. She is a single unmarried
b. She is single
c. She is unmarried single
d. She is a unmarried person


Correct Answer: b

Solution:
Option (b) is correct. It is concise and grammatically correct: 'She is single.'

Explanation:
The words 'single' and 'unmarried' convey the same meaning, so using both together (as in options a and c) is redundant and ungrammatical. Option (d) is incorrect because the article should be 'an' before a vowel sound ('an unmarried person'). Therefore the best and correct sentence is option (b).
