C. Men hardly believe a liar, do we?

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Question:
No one called the manager, -?

Options:
ক. didn't he
খ. did they
গ. don't they
ঘ. didn't they


Correct Answer: খ

Solution:
Sentence: "No one called the manager." This is a negative indefinite subject (no one) with past tense verb 'called'. The tag must be positive and use the past tense auxiliary 'did' plus an appropriate pronoun. Neutral pronoun commonly used in tag questions is 'they'. So the correct tag is 'did they?'.

Explanation:
Indefinite negatives like 'no one', 'nobody' make the main clause negative, so the tag is positive. The main clause is past simple ('called'), so the auxiliary for the tag is 'did' (positive form). English tag convention often uses 'they' as a neutral pronoun: "No one called the manager, did they?"
