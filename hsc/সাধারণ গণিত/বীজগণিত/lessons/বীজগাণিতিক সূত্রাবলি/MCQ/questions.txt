Question:
যদি [math]x^4-x^2+1=0[/math] হয়, তবে [math]x^3+\frac{1}{x^3} = ?[/math]

Options:
A. 3
B. 2
C. 1
D. 0


Correct Answer: ঘ

Solution:
[math]\begin{array}{l} x^{4}-x^{2}+1=0 \\\Rightarrow x^{4}+1=x^{2} \Rightarrow \frac{x^{4}+1}{x^{2}}=1 \\\Rightarrow \frac{x^{4}}{x^{2}}+\frac{1}{x^{2}}=1 \Rightarrow x^{2}+\frac{1}{x^{2}}=1\end{array}[/math]
 [math]\begin{array}{l}\Rightarrow\left(x+\frac{1}{x}\right)^{2}-2 x \cdot \frac{1}{x}=1 \\\Rightarrow\left(x+\frac{1}{x}\right)^{2}-2=1 \Rightarrow\left(x+\frac{1}{x}\right)^{2}=1+2 \\\Rightarrow\left(x+\frac{1}{x}\right)^{2}=3 \Rightarrow x+\frac{1}{x}=\sqrt{3}\end{array}[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow (x+\frac{1}{x})^3 = (\sqrt{3})^3 \quad[/math]
 [উভয়কক্ষে ঘন করে][math]\begin{array}{l}\Rightarrow x^{3}+\frac{1}{x^{3}}+3 x \cdot \frac{1}{x}\left(x+\frac{1}{x}\right)=3 \sqrt{3} \\\Rightarrow x^{3}+\frac{1}{x^{3}}+3\left(x+\frac{1}{x}\right)=3 \sqrt{3} \\\Rightarrow x^{3}+\frac{1}{x^{3}}+3 \sqrt{3}=3 \sqrt{3} \Rightarrow x^{3}+\frac{1}{x^{3}}=0\end{array}[/math]

Question:
If [math]a^4 + a^2b^2 + b^4 = a^2 + ab + b^2 =3[/math], What is the value of [math]a^2 + b^2?[/math]

Options:
A. 4
B. 6
C. 3
D. 2


Correct Answer: ঘ

Solution:
দেওয়া আছে,[math]\begin{aligned}&a^4+a^2b^2+b^4=a^2+ab+b^2\\\Rightarrow &\left(a^2\right)^2+2\cdot a^2\cdot b^2+\left(b^2\right)^2-a^2b^2=a^2+ab+b^2\\\Rightarrow &\left(a^2+b^2\right)^2-(ab)^2=a^2+ab+b^2\\\Rightarrow &\left(a^2+ab+b^2\right)\left(a^2-ab+b^2\right)=\left(a^2+ab+b^2\right)\\\Rightarrow & \:a^2-ab+b^2=1\\\Rightarrow & \:ab=\left(a^2+b^2\right)-1\\\end{aligned}[/math]
আবার, [math]a^2+ab+b^2 = 3[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow (a^2+b^2) + (a^2+b^2-1) = 3[/math]
 [ab এর মান বসিয়ে][math]\Rightarrow 2(a^2+b^2) =4 \quad \therefore a^2+b^2 = 2[/math]
