Question:
x+y=12x-y=2xy এর মান কত?

Options:


Correct Answer: গ

Solution:
[math]\begin{array}{l}x y=\left(\frac{x+y}{2}\right)^{2}-\left(\frac{x-y}{2}\right)^{2} \\=\left(\frac{12}{2}\right)^{2}-\left(\frac{2}{2}\right)^{2}=(6)^{2}-(1)^{2}=36-1=3\end{array}[/math]

Explanation:
ব্যাখ্যা: ধরা যাক [math]x+y=12x-y=2xy=k[/math]. প্রথম দুটির থেকে [math]2y=11x\Rightarrow y=\tfrac{11}{2}x[/math], তাই [math]k=x+y=\tfrac{13}{2}x[/math] এবং [math]2xy=2\cdot\frac{2}{13}k\cdot\frac{11}{13}k=\frac{44}{169}k^2=k[/math], ফলে [math]k=0[/math] অথবা [math]k=\tfrac{169}{44}[/math]. অতএব [math]xy=\tfrac{k}{2}=0\quad\text{অথবা}\quad \tfrac{169}{88}[/math], এবং বিকল্প গ (C) হচ্ছে [math]\tfrac{169}{88}[/math]।

Question:
[math]a^2[/math]+ [math]b^2[/math] = 7 এবং ab = 10 হলে, [math](a + b)^2[/math] = কত?

Options:


Correct Answer: গ

Solution:
[math]\begin{array}{l}(a+b)^{2}=a^{2}+b^{2}+2 a b \\=7+2 \times 10=7+20=27\end{array}[/math]

Explanation:
ব্যাখ্যা: আমরা সূত্র ব্যবহার করি [math](a+b)^2 = a^2 + b^2 + 2ab[/math]. দেয়া আছে [math]a^2+b^2=7[/math] এবং [math]ab=10[/math], তাই [math](a+b)^2 = 7 + 2\times10 = 27[/math], অতএব সঠিক উত্তরটি C।

Question:
[math]x-\frac{1}{x}=4 c x^{2}+\frac{1}{x 2}[/math]

Options:


Correct Answer: ক

Solution:
[math]\begin{array}{l}x^{2}+\frac{1}{x 2}=\left(x-\frac{1}{x}\right)^{2}+2 \cdot x \cdot \frac{1}{x} \\=(4)^{2}+2 \\=16+2=18\end{array}[/math]

Explanation:
Square both sides and use the identity [math](x-\frac{1}{x})^2 = x^2+\frac{1}{x^2}-2[/math]. Since [math](x-\frac{1}{x})=4[/math], we get [math]x^2+\frac{1}{x^2}=4^2+2=18[/math], so option A is correct.
