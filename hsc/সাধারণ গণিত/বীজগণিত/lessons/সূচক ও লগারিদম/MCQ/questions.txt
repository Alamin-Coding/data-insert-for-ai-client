Question #28 (ID: 354017)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Type: MCQ | Book: সাধারণ গণিত | Years: ৪০ | Boards:  | Tags: বিসিএস প্রিলি '৪৩ তম

Question:
[math]x^{x \sqrt{x}}=(x \sqrt{x})^{x}[/math] হলে, [math]x[/math] এর মান কত?

Options:
A. [math]\frac{3}{2}[/math]
B. [math]\frac{4}{5}[/math]
C. [math]\frac{9}{4}[/math]
D. [math]\frac{2}{3}[/math]


Correct Answer: গ

Solution:
[math]\begin{array}{l} x^{x \sqrt{x}}=(x \sqrt{x})^{x} \\\Rightarrow x^{x \cdot x^{\frac{1}{2}}}=\left\{x\left(1+\frac{1}{2}\right)\right\}^{x} \Rightarrow x^{x}\left(1+\frac{1}{2}\right)=x^{\frac{3}{2} x} \\\Rightarrow x^{x^{\frac{3}{2}}}=x^{\frac{3}{2} x} \Rightarrow x^{\frac{3}{2}}=\frac{3}{2} x \Rightarrow \frac{x^{\frac{3}{2}}}{x}=\frac{3}{2} \\\Rightarrow x^{\left(\frac{3}{2}-1\right)}=\frac{3}{2} \Rightarrow x\left(\frac{3-2}{2}\right)=\frac{3}{2} \\\Rightarrow x^{\frac{1}{2}}=\frac{3}{2} \Rightarrow\left(x^{\frac{1}{2}}\right)^{2}=\left(\frac{3}{2}\right)^{2} \therefore x=\frac{9}{4}\end{array}[/math]

[math]\sqrt[m n]{\frac{x^{m}}{x^{n}}} \sqrt[n]{\frac{x^{n}}{x^{l}}} \sqrt[m l]{\frac{x^{l}}{x^{m}}}[/math]

B. [math]x^m[/math]
C. [math]x^l[/math]
D. 1


Correct Answer: ঘ

Solution:
[math]\begin{array}{l}\sqrt[m n]{\frac{x^{m}}{x^{n}}} \sqrt[n l]{\frac{x^{n}}{x^{l}}} \sqrt[m l]{\frac{x^{l}}{x^{m}}} \\=\left(\frac{\mathrm{x}^{\mathrm{m}}}{\mathrm{x}^{\mathrm{n}}}\right)^{\frac{1}{\mathrm{mn}}} \times\left(\frac{\mathrm{x}^{\mathrm{n}}}{\mathrm{x}^{l}}\right)^{\frac{1}{\mathrm{n} l}}\times\left(\frac{\mathrm{x}^{l}}{\mathrm{x}^{\mathrm{m}}}\right)^{\frac{1}{\mathrm{~m} l}} \\=\left(\mathrm{x}^{\mathrm{m}-\mathrm{n}}\right)^{\frac{1}{\mathrm{mn}}} \times\left(\mathrm{x}^{\mathrm{n}-l}\right)^{\frac{1}{\mathrm{n} l}} \times\left(\mathrm{x}^{1-m}\right)^{\frac{1}{\mathrm{~m} l}} \\=\mathrm{x}^{\frac{\mathrm{m}-\mathrm{n}}{\mathrm{mn}}} \cdot \mathrm{x}^{\frac{\mathrm{n}-l}{\mathrm{n} l}} \cdot \mathrm{x}^{\frac{l-\mathrm{m}}{l \mathrm{~m}}}=\mathrm{x}^{\frac{\mathrm{m}-\mathrm{n}}{\mathrm{mn}}+\frac{\mathrm{n}-l}{\mathrm{n} l}+\frac{l-\mathrm{m}}{l \mathrm{~m}}} \\=\mathrm{x}^{\frac{l \mathrm{~m}-\mathrm{ln}+\mathrm{mn}-\mathrm{lm}+\mathrm{ln}-\mathrm{mn}}{l \mathrm{mn}}}=\mathrm{x}^{\frac{0}{l \mathrm{mn}}}=\mathrm{x}^{0}=1 \\\end{array}[/math]

Question:
[math]\sqrt[3]{2}: \sqrt[3]{4}=7: x[/math]

Options:
A. 14
B. 10
C. [math]7\sqrt[3]{2}[/math]
D. [math]7\sqrt{2}[/math]
E. কোনোটিই নয়


Correct Answer: গ

Solution:
দেওয়া আছে, [math]\sqrt[3]{2}: \sqrt[3]{4}=7: x[/math]
 [math]\begin{array}{l}\Rightarrow \frac{2^{\frac{1}{3}}}{4^{\frac{1}{3}}}=\frac{7}{x} \Rightarrow \frac{2^{\frac{1}{3}}}{\frac{2}{2^{\frac{2}{3}}}}=\frac{7}{x} \\\Rightarrow x=7 \times \frac{2^{\frac{2}{3}}}{2^{\frac{1}{3}}}=7 \times 2^{\frac{2}{3}-\frac{1}{3}}=7 \times 2^{\frac{1}{3}} \\\therefore x=7 \times \sqrt[3]{2}\end{array}[/math]

খ. 1

[math]4^{x+1}=32[/math] হলে [math]x[/math] এর মান কত?

ক. [math]\frac{1}{2}[/math]
খ. 1
গ. [math]\frac{3}{2}[/math]
ঘ. [math]\frac{2}{3}[/math]


Correct Answer: গ

Solution:
[math]\begin{array}{l} 4^{x+1}=32 \\\Rightarrow\left(2^{2}\right)^{x+1}=2^{5} \\\Rightarrow 2^{2 x+2}=2^{5} \Rightarrow 2 x+2=5 \\\Rightarrow 2 x=3 \therefore x=\frac{3}{2}\end{array}[/math]
