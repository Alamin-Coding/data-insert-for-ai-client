Question:
[math]f(x)= x^2-5x+6[/math] এবং [math]f(x)=0[/math] হলে, [math]x=[/math] কত?

Options:
ক. 2, 3
খ. -2, 3
গ. 2, -3
ঘ. 6, -1


Correct Answer: ক

Solution:
[math]\begin{array}{l} f(x)=x^{2}-5 x+6 \\\Rightarrow 0=x^{2}-5 x+6 \\\Rightarrow x^{2}-5 x+6=0 \\\Rightarrow x^{2}-3 x-2 x+6=0 \\\Rightarrow x(x-3)-2(x-3)=0 \\\Rightarrow(x-3)(x-2)=0\end{array}[/math]
 [math]\therefore x = 3[/math]
 অথবা, [math]x =2[/math]

Question:
[math](a-b)^3+(b-c)^3+(c-a)^3[/math] কে উৎপাদকে বিশ্লেষণ করুন।

Options:
ক. [math]9(a-b)(b-c)(c-a)[/math]
খ. [math]3(a-b)(b-c)(c-a)[/math]
গ. [math]2(a-b)(b-c)(c-a)[/math]
ঘ. [math](a-b)(b-c)(c-a)[/math]


Correct Answer: খ

Solution:
[math]\begin{aligned} & (a-b)^{3}+(b-c)^{3}+(c-a)^{3} \\= & \:a^{3}-3 a b(a-b)-b^{3}+b^{3}-3 b c(b-c) -c^{3}+c^{3}-3 c a(c-a)-a^{3} \\= & -3\{a b(a-b)+b c(b-c)+c a(c-a)\} \\= & -3\left\{a b(a-b)+b^{2} c-b c^{2}+c^{2} a-c a^{2}\right\} \\= & -3\left\{a b(a-b)+b^{2} c-c^{2}-c^{2}+c^{2} a\right\} \\= & -3\left\{a b(a-b)+c\left(b^{2}-a^{2}\right)+c^{2}(a-b)\right\} \\= & -3\left\{a b(a-b)+c(b+a)(b-a)+c^{2}(a-b)\right\} \\= & -3\left\{a b(a-b)-c(a+b)(a-b)+c^{2}(a-b)\right\} \\= & -3(a-b)\left\{a b-c a-b c+c^{2}\right\} \\= & -3(a-b)\{a(b-c)-c(b-c)\} \\= & -3(a-b)(b-c)(a-c) \\= & 3(a-b)(b-c)(c-a)\end{aligned}[/math]

Question:
কোনটি [math]2x^4-5x^3+6x^2-5x+2[/math] -এর একটি উৎপাদক?

Options:
ক. x + 1
খ. x - 1
গ. x + 2
ঘ. x - 2


Correct Answer: খ

Solution:
Option check :(A). [math]x + 1[/math]
 উৎপাদক হলে [math]x+ 1 = 0[/math]
 বা [math]x = -1[/math]
 দ্বারা রাশিটির মান 0 হবে।[math]x = -1[/math]
 হলে, [math]2(-1)^4-5(-1)^3+6(-1)^2-5(-1)+2[/math]
 [math]\begin{array}{l} & \qquad \qquad\Rightarrow 2+5+6+5+2 \\ & \qquad \qquad\Rightarrow 20 
eq 0\end{array}[/math]
(B). [math]x - 1[/math]
 উৎপাদক হলে [math]x- 1 = 0[/math]
 বা [math]x -1[/math]
 দ্বারা রাশিটির মান 0 হবে।[math]x = 1[/math]
 হলে, [math]2 \times(1)^{4}-5 \times 1^{3}+6 \times 1^{2}-5 \times 1+2[/math]
 [math]\begin{array}{l}& \qquad \qquad=2-5+6-5+2 \\& \qquad \qquad =10-10=0\end{array}[/math]
 [math]\therefore (x-1),[/math]
 প্রদত্ত রাশিটির উৎপাদক।
