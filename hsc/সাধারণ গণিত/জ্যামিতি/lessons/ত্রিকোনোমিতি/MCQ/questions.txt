Question:

Options:
A. [math]-\frac{1}{2}[/math]
B. [math]\frac{1}{2}[/math]

Correct Answer: ঘ

Solution:
[math]\begin{aligned}&\tan \frac{\pi }{12}\cdot \tan \frac{5\pi }{12}\cdot \tan \frac{7\pi }{12}\cdot \tan \frac{11\pi }{12}\\=&\tan \frac{\pi }{12}\cdot \tan \frac{5\pi }{12}\cdot \tan \left(\frac{6\pi +\pi }{12}\right)\cdot \tan \left(\frac{6\pi +5\pi }{12}\right)\\=&\tan \frac{\pi }{12}\cdot \tan \frac{5\pi }{12}\cdot \tan \left(\frac{\pi }{2}+\frac{\pi }{12}\right)\cdot \tan \left(\frac{\pi }{2}+\frac{5\pi }{12}\right)\\=&\tan \frac{\pi }{12}\cdot \tan \frac{5\pi }{12}\cdot \left(-\cot \frac{\pi }{12}\right)\cdot \left(-\cot \frac{5\pi }{12}\right)\\=&\left(\tan \frac{\pi }{12}\cdot \cot \frac{\pi }{12}\right)\times \left(\tan \frac{5\pi }{12}\times \cot \frac{5\pi }{12}\right)\\=&1\times 1=1\end{aligned}[/math]

Question:

B. 2
C. [math]\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}[/math]
D. [math]\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}[/math]


Correct Answer: ক

Solution:

[Image]
[math]\cos \theta=\frac{2}{\sqrt{5}}[/math]

[Image]
পীথাগোরাসের উপপাদ্য অনুসারে,[math]\begin{array}{l}\triangle \mathrm{ABC} \: \text{এ} \: A B^{2}=A C^{2}+B C^{2} \\\quad \Rightarrow(\sqrt{5})^{2}=A C^{2}+2^{2} \Rightarrow A C^{2}=5-4=1 \\\quad \therefore A C=1\end{array}[/math]

[Image]
বিকল্প সমাধান : [math]\cos \theta=\frac{2}{\sqrt{5}} \Rightarrow \cos ^{2} \theta=\frac{4}{5}[/math]
 [math]\begin{aligned}& \sin ^{2} \theta+\cos ^{2} \theta=1 \\\Rightarrow & \sin ^{2} \theta+\frac{4}{5}=1 \Rightarrow \sin ^{2} \theta=1-\frac{4}{5}=\frac{1}{5} \\\therefore & \sin \theta=\frac{1}{\sqrt{5}}\end{aligned}[/math]
এখন, [math]\tan \theta=\frac{\sin \theta}{\cos \theta}=\frac{\frac{1}{\sqrt{5}}}{\frac{2}{\sqrt{5}}}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{5}} \times \frac{\sqrt{5}}{2}=\frac{1}{2}[/math]

Question:
[math]\cos ^{-1} x+\cos ^{-1} y=\frac{\pi}{2}[/math] হলে, [math]x^2+y^2 = ?[/math]

Options:
A. [math]\sqrt{\frac{3}{2}}[/math]
B. [math]\frac{1}{2}[/math]
C. 1
D. 0


Correct Answer: গ

Solution:
[math]\begin{array}{l} \cos ^{-1} x+\cos ^{-1} y=\frac{\pi}{2} \\\Rightarrow \cos ^{-1}\left[x y-\sqrt{\left(1-x^{2}\right)\left(1-y^{2}\right)}\right]=\frac{\pi}{2} \\\Rightarrow x y-\sqrt{\left(1-x^{2}\right)\left(1-y^{2}\right)}=0 \\\Rightarrow x y=\sqrt{\left(1-x^{2}\right)\left(1-y^{2}\right)} \\\Rightarrow x^{2} y^{2}=1-x^{2}-y^{2}+x^{2} y^{2} \\\therefore x^{2}+y^{2}=1\end{array}[/math]
বিকল্প সমাধান : [math]\cos ^{-1} x+\cos ^{-1} y=\frac{\pi}{2}[/math]
 [math]\begin{array}{l}\Rightarrow \cos ^{-1} x=\left(\frac{\pi}{2}-\cos ^{-1} y\right) \\\Rightarrow x=\cos \left(\frac{\pi}{2}-\cos ^{-1} y\right) \\\Rightarrow x=\cos \frac{\pi}{2} \cos \cos ^{-1}(y)+\sin \frac{\pi}{2} \sin \left(\cos ^{-1} y\right) \\\Rightarrow x=0+1 \times \sqrt{\left(1-y^{2}\right)} \\\Rightarrow x^{2}=1-y^{2} \\\therefore x^{2}+y^{2}=1\end{array}[/math]

Question:
[math]sinA + cosA = sinB + cosB[/math] হলে, [math]A + B = ?[/math]

Options:
A. [math]\pi[/math]
B. [math]2\pi[/math]
C. [math]\frac{\pi}{2}[/math]
D. [math]\frac{\pi}{4}[/math]


Correct Answer: গ

Solution:
[math]\sin A + \cos A = \sin B + \cos B[/math]
 [math]\begin{array}{l}\Rightarrow \sin A-\sin B=\cos B-\cos A \\\Rightarrow 2 \sin \frac{A-B}{2} \cos \frac{A+B}{2}=2 \sin \frac{B+A}{2} \sin \frac{A-B}{2}\end{array}[/math]
 [math]\bigg[[/math]
 সূত্র : [math]\begin{array}{l}\sin C-\sin D=2 \cos \frac{C+D}{2} \sin \frac{C-D}{2}, \\\cos C-\cos D=2 \sin \frac{C+D}{2} \sin \frac{D-C}{2}\end{array}[/math]
 [math]\bigg][/math]
 [math]\begin{array}{l}\Rightarrow 2 \cos \frac{A+B}{2}=2 \sin \frac{A+B}{2} \\\Rightarrow \frac{\sin \frac{A+B}{2}}{\cos \frac{A+B}{2}}=1 \Rightarrow \tan \frac{A+B}{2}=\tan \frac{\pi}{4} \\\Rightarrow \frac{A+B}{2}=\frac{\pi}{4} \therefore A+B=\frac{\pi}{2}\end{array}[/math]
