[math]I_C=\beta I_B=19\times1.5\ \text{mA}=28.5\ \text{mA},\quad I_E=I_C+I_B=(19+1)\times1.5\ \text{mA}=30.0\ \text{mA}[/math]; কারণ [math]\alpha\approx I_C/I_E[/math]।
