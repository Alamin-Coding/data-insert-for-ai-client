Options:
ক. 120.5 days
খ. 222.6 days

Solution:
[math]\mathrm{T}_{\frac{1}{2}}=\frac{0.693}{\lambda}[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow \lambda=\frac{0.693}{\mathrm{~T} \frac{1}{2}}[/math]
 [math]=\frac{0.693}{20}=0.03465 \mathrm{~d}^{-1}[/math]
এখানে, N = x[math]\mathrm{N}_{0}=70 \mathrm{x}[/math]
আমরা জানি, [math]\mathrm{N}=\mathrm{N}_{0} \mathrm{e}^{-\lambda t}[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow x=70 \mathrm{xe}^{-\lambda t} \Rightarrow 1[/math]
 [math]=70 \mathrm{e}^{-\lambda t} \Rightarrow 0.01428=\mathrm{e}^{-\lambda t}[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow \ln (0.01428)[/math]
 [math]=-\lambda t \Rightarrow-4.02485[/math]
 [math]=-\lambda t[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow \mathrm{t}=\frac{4.2485}{\lambda}[/math]
 [math]=\frac{4.2485}{0.03465}[/math]
 [math]=122.6 \text { day }[/math]
