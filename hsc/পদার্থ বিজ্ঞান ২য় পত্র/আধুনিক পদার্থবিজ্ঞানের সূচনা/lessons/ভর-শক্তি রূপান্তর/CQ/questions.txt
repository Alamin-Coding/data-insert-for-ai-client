Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:
[math]41 \ eV[/math]
 [math]\\[/math]
 [math][/math]
এখানে,ইলেকট্রনের বেগ, [math]v=3.8 \times 10^{6}ms^{-1}[/math]
ইলেকট্রনের ভর, [math]m=9.11 \times 10^{-31}kg[/math]
গতিশক্তি, [math]E_{k}=?[/math]
 [math]\\[/math]
আমরা জানি,[math]\begin{aligned} \mathrm{E}_{\mathrm{k}} & =\frac{1}{2} \mathrm{mv}^{2} \\ & =\frac{1}{2} \times 9.11 \times 10^{-31} \mathrm{~kg} \times\left(3.8 \times 10^{6} \mathrm{~ms}^{-1}\right)^{2} \\ & =6.57 \times 10^{-18} \mathrm{~J} \\ & =\frac{6.57 \times 10^{-18}}{1.6 \times 10^{-19}} \mathrm{eV} \\ & =41 \mathrm{eV} \text { (Ans.) } \end{aligned}[/math]


Explanation:
ব্যাখ্যা: একটি কণার গতিশক্তি [math]K=\frac{1}{2}mv^2[/math] দ্বারা নির্ণয় করা হয়, তাই এখানে [math]K=\frac{1}{2}\times 9.1\times10^{-31}\times(3.8\times10^{6})^2\approx6.58\times10^{-18}\ \text{J}[/math]. জুলকে ইলেকট্রন ভোল্টে রূপান্তর করলে [math]K=\dfrac{6.58\times10^{-18}}{1.6\times10^{-19}}\approx41.1\ \text{eV}[/math], তাই গতিশক্তি প্রায় [math]41\ \text{eV}[/math].

Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:
[math]4.44 \times 10^{9}kg[/math]
 [math]\\[/math]
 [math][/math]
এখানে,নিশ্চল অবস্থায় শক্তি, [math]\mathrm{E}_{0}=4.0 \times 10^{26} \mathrm{~J}[/math]
আলোর বেগ, [math]\mathrm{c}=3 \times 10^{8} \mathrm{~ms}^{-1}[/math]
নিশ্চল ভর, [math]m_{0}=?[/math]
 [math]\\[/math]
আমরা জানি,[math]\begin{aligned} E_{0} & =m_{0} \mathrm{c}^{2} \\ \therefore m_{0} & =\frac{E_{0}}{c^{2}} \\ & =\frac{4.0 \times 10^{26} \mathrm{~J}}{\left(3 \times 10^{8} \mathrm{~ms}^{-1}\right)^{2}} \\ & =4.44 \times 10^{9} \mathrm{~kg} \text { (Ans.) } \end{aligned}[/math]


Explanation:
ব্যাখ্যা: বিশ্রাম শক্তি এবং ভরের সম্পর্ক হল [math]E=mc^{2}[/math], তাই ভর [math]m=E/c^{2}[/math]। এখানে [math]m=\dfrac{4.0\times10^{26}}{(3.0\times10^{8})^{2}}=\dfrac{4.0\times10^{26}}{9.0\times10^{16}}=4.44\times10^{9}\ \text{kg}\approx4.4\times10^{9}\ \text{kg}[/math]।

Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:
[math]2.236 \times 10^{8}ms^{-1}[/math]
 [math]\\[/math]
 [math][/math]
দেওয়া আছে,[math]\begin{array}{l} \mathrm{mc}^{2}=1.5 \mathrm{~m}_{0} \mathrm{c}^{2} \\ \Rightarrow \frac{\mathrm{m}}{\mathrm{m}_{0}}=1.5 \end{array}[/math]
দ্রুতি, [math]v=?[/math]
 [math]\\[/math]
আমরা জানি,[math]\begin{array}{l} \Rightarrow m=\frac{m_{0}}{\sqrt{1-\frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}}}} \\ \Rightarrow \frac{m}{m_{0}}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-\frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}}}} \\ \Rightarrow 1.5=\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-\frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}}}} \\ \Rightarrow (1.5)^{2}=\frac{1}{1-\frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}}} \\ \Rightarrow 1-\frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}}=\frac{1}{(1.5)^{2}} \\ \Rightarrow \frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}}=1-\frac{1}{(1.5)^{2}} \\ \Rightarrow \frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}}=0.5555 \\ \Rightarrow \frac{\mathrm{v}}{\mathrm{c}}=0.7453 \\ \Rightarrow v=0.7453 c \\ =0.7453 \times 3 \times 10^{8} \\ =2.236 \times 10^{8} \mathrm{~ms}^{-1} (Ans.)\\ \end{array}[/math]


Explanation:
ব্যাখ্যা: কণার মোট শক্তি [math]E=\gamma mc^2[/math] এবং স্থির শক্তি [math]E_0=mc^2[/math], তাই [math]\gamma=E/E_0=1.5=3/2[/math]. [math]\gamma=1/\sqrt{1-v^2/c^2}=3/2\Rightarrow\sqrt{1-v^2/c^2}=2/3\Rightarrow v^2/c^2=5/9[/math], ফলে [math]v=(\sqrt{5}/3)\,c\approx0.745\,c[/math].

আমরা জানি,[math]\begin{array}{l} \mathrm{m}=\frac{\mathrm{m}^{2}}{\sqrt{1-\frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}}}} \\ \Rightarrow \frac{\mathrm{m}}{\mathrm{m}_{0}}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-\frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}}}} \\ \Rightarrow 1.5=\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-\frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}}}} \\ \Rightarrow 1-\frac{1}{c^{2}}=\frac{1}{(1.5)^{2}} \\ \Rightarrow \frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}}=1-\frac{1}{(1.5)^{2}} \\ \Rightarrow \frac{v^{2}}{c^{2}} \\ \Rightarrow \frac{\mathrm{v}}{\mathrm{c}}=0.5555 \\ \Rightarrow \frac{v}{v}=0.7453 \mathrm{c} \\ =0.7453 \times 3 \times 10^{2} \\ =2.236 \times 10^{8} \mathrm{~ms}^{-1} (Ans.) \end{array}[/math]

ব্যাখ্যা: মোট শক্তি [math]E=\gamma mc^2[/math] এবং স্থির শক্তি [math]E_0=mc^2[/math] হওয়ায় [math]E=1.5E_0[/math] থেকে [math]\gamma=1.5=3/2[/math]. কারণ [math]\gamma=1/\sqrt{1-v^2/c^2}=3/2[/math] হলে [math]v/c=\sqrt{1-(2/3)^2}=\sqrt{5}/3\approx0.745\,c[/math], তাই কণাটির দ্রুতি [math](\sqrt{5}/3)c\approx0.745c[/math].
