Question:
নিচের উদ্দীপকের আলোকে প্রশ্নের উত্তর দাও:

[math]A=\left[\begin{array}{cc}3 & -2 \\2 & 2\end{array}\right], D=\left[\begin{array}{rrr}x & 0 & 0 \\2 & 4 & 1 \\3 & -2 & 0\end{array}\right][/math]

x এর কোন মানের জন্য [math]|\mathbf{A}|=|\mathbf{D}|[/math] হবে ?

Options:
ক. 1
খ. 2
গ. -2
ঘ. 5


Correct Answer: ঘ

Solution:
[math]|A| =3.2-2(-2)=10[/math]
 [math]|D| =\left|\begin{array}{lrr}x & 0 & 0 \\2 & 4 & 1 \\3 & -2 & 0\end{array}\right|[/math]
 [math]=x\left|\begin{array}{cc}4 & 1 \\-2 & 0\end{array}\right|[/math]
 [math]=x(0+2)=2 x[/math]
প্রশ্নমতে [math]2 x=10[/math]
 [math]\therefore x=5[/math]

Question:
[math]\left|\begin{array}{lll}2 & 2 & x \\4 & 4 & 4 \\3 & x & 3\end{array}\right|=0[/math] হলে [math]\mathrm x[/math] এর মান কত?

Options:
A. [math]2,4[/math]
B. [math]4,2[/math]
C. [math]2,3[/math]
D. [math]3,4[/math]


Correct Answer: গ

Solution:
[math]\left|\begin{array}{lll}2 & 2 & x \\4 & 4 & 4 \\3 & x & 3\end{array}\right|[/math]
 [math]=0[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow 2(12-4 x)[/math]
 [math]-2(12-12)+x(4 x-12)[/math]
 [math]=0[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow 24-8 x+4 x^{2}-12 x[/math]
 [math]=0[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow x^{2}-5[/math]
 [math]x+6=0[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow (x-3)(x-2)[/math]
 [math]=0[/math]
 [math]\therefore \mathrm{x}[/math]
 [math]=2,3[/math]

Question:
[math]\left|\begin{array}{cc}a-5 & 3 \\-3 & a+5\end{array}\right|[/math] এর মান [math]0[/math] হলে, [math]\mathrm a[/math] এর মান-

Options:
A. [math]4,-4[/math]
B. [math]\sqrt{34},-\sqrt{34}[/math]
C. [math]5,3[/math]
D. [math]0,4[/math]


Correct Answer: ক

Solution:
[math]\left|\begin{array}{cc}a-5 & 3 \\-3 & a+5\end{array}\right|=0[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow (a-5)(a+5)+9=0[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow \mathrm{a}^{2}-25+9=0[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow \mathrm{a}^{2}-16=0 \quad[/math]
 [math]\therefore \mathrm{a}[/math]
 [math]= \pm 4[/math]
