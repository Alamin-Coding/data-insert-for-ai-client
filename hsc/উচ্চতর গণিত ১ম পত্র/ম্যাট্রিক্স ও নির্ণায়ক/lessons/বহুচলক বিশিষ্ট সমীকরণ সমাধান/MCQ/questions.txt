Options:
A. [math](2,0)[/math]
B. [math](2,2)[/math]
C. [math](2,4)[/math]
D. [math](4,-3)[/math]


Correct Answer: ক

Solution:

 [math]\Rightarrow\left[\begin{array}{l}3 x+y \\2 x+0\end{array}\right][/math]
 [math]=\left[\begin{array}{l}6 \\4\end{array}\right][/math]
 [math]2 x=4[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow x=2,3 x+y=6[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow y=0[/math]
 [math]\therefore(x, y)[/math]
 [math]=(2,0)[/math]
