Question:
একটি সামন্তরিকের সন্নিহিত বাহু দুটি যথাক্রমে [math]\vec{A}=3 \hat{i}+\hat{j}-2 \hat{k}[/math] এবং [math]\vec{B}=\hat{i}-3 \hat{j}+4 \hat{k}[/math]  হলে সামন্তরিকের ক্ষেত্রফল-

Options:
ক. <span class="math-tex">\(10 \sqrt{2}\)</span> বর্গ একক
খ. &nbsp;<span class="math-tex">\(10 \sqrt{3}\)</span> বর্গ একক
গ. &nbsp;<span class="math-tex">\(3 \sqrt{10}\)</span> বর্গ একক
ঘ. &nbsp;<span class="math-tex">\(2\sqrt{10}\)</span> বর্গ একক


Correct Answer: খ

Solution:
\(\begin{aligned}&amp; \text {  } \vec{A} \times \vec{B}=\left|\begin{array}{rrr}\hat{i} &amp; \hat{j} &amp; \hat{k} \\3 &amp; 1 &amp; -2 \\1 &amp; -3 &amp; 4\end{array}\right| \\\end{aligned}\)\(=\hat{\mathrm{i}}(4-6)-\hat{\mathrm{j}}(12+2)+\hat{\mathrm{k}}(-9-1) \\=-2 \hat{i}-14 \hat{j}-10 \hat{k} \\\)\(\therefore \)ক্ষেত্রফল\(=|\overrightarrow{\mathrm{A}} \times \overrightarrow{\mathrm{B}}|=\sqrt{(-2)^2+(-14)^2+(-10)^2} \\\)\(=\sqrt{300}=10 \sqrt{3} \) বর্গ একক

Question:

Options:
ক. <span class="math-tex">\(3 \sqrt{3}\)</span>
খ. <span class="math-tex">\(6 \sqrt{3}\)</span>
গ. <span class="math-tex">\( 6 \sqrt{6}\)</span>
ঘ. <span class="math-tex">\(3 \sqrt{6}\)</span>


Correct Answer: গ

Solution:
\(\vec{a} \times \vec{b}=\left|\begin{array}{ccc}\hat{i} &amp; \hat{j} &amp; \hat{k} \\4 &amp; -3 &amp; 2 \\2 &amp; -3 &amp; 4\end{array}\right|=-6 \hat{\mathrm{i}}-12 \hat{j}-6 \hat{\mathrm{k}} \\\)\(\therefore \)ক্ষেত্রফল =\(\overrightarrow{\mathrm{a}} \times \overrightarrow{\mathrm{b}}|=\sqrt{6^2+12^2+6^2}=6 \sqrt{1+2^2+1} \\\) \(=6 \sqrt{6}\)বর্গএকক।

Options:
ক. <span class="math-tex">\(\frac{3}{2}\)</span>
খ. <span class="math-tex">\(\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\)</span>
গ. <span class="math-tex">\( \frac{\sqrt{5}}{2}\)</span>
