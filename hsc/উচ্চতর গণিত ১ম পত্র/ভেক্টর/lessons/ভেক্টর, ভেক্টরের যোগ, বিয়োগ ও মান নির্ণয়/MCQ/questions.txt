Question:
[math]\overrightarrow{\mathbf{A}}=\hat{\mathbf{i}}+2 \hat{\mathbf{j}}+3 \hat{\mathbf{k}}[/math]এবং [math]\ \overrightarrow{\mathrm{B}}=3 \hat{\mathbf{i}}+2 \hat{\mathbf{j}}-\hat{\mathbf{k}}[/math]

[math]i. \overrightarrow{\mathrm{A}}-\overrightarrow{\mathrm{B}}=-2 \hat{\mathbf{i}}+4 \hat{\mathbf{k}}\\
ii. |\overrightarrow{\mathbf{A}} \cdot \overrightarrow{\mathrm{B}}|=4\\
iii. |\overrightarrow{\mathbf{A}} \times \overrightarrow{\mathbf{B}}|=4[/math]

Options:
ক. i, ii
খ. ii, iii
গ. i,iii
ঘ. i,ii,iii


Correct Answer: ক

Solution:
\(\overrightarrow{\mathrm{A}}-\overrightarrow{\mathrm{B}} =\hat{\mathrm{i}}+2 \hat{\mathrm{j}}+3 \hat{\mathrm{k}}-3 \hat{\mathrm{i}}+2 \hat{\mathrm{j}}+\hat{\mathrm{k}}\)=-2 \hat{\mathrm{i}}+4 \hat{\mathrm{k}} \text { (i) সঠিক\(\overrightarrow{\mathrm{A}} \cdot \overrightarrow{\mathrm{B}}=1 \times 3+2 \times 2+3 \times(-1)=3+4-3=4\\\therefore|\overrightarrow{\mathrm{A}} \cdot \overrightarrow{\mathrm{B}}|=|4|=4 ...(ii)\)(ii) নং সঠিক\(\begin{aligned}\overrightarrow{\mathrm{A}} \times \overrightarrow{\mathrm{B}}=\left|\begin{array}{ccc}\hat{\mathrm{i}} &amp; \hat{\mathrm{j}} &amp; \hat{\mathrm{k}} \\1 &amp; 2 &amp; 3 \\3 &amp; 2 &amp; -1\end{array}\right| &amp; =\hat{\mathrm{i}}(-2-6)-\hat{\mathrm{j}}(-1-9)+\hat{\mathrm{k}}(2-6) \\&amp; =-8 \hat{\mathrm{i}}+10 \hat{\mathrm{j}}-4 \hat{\mathrm{k}}\end{aligned}\)\(​​​​​​​\therefore|\overrightarrow{\mathrm{A}} \times \overrightarrow{\mathrm{B}}|=\sqrt{(-8)^2+10^2+(-4)^2}=\sqrt{180}=6 \sqrt{5}\)যা সঠিক নয়।

[math]\vec{r}=2 t \hat{i}+3 t^2 \hat{j}[/math] হচ্ছে একটি বিন্দুবস্তুর অবস্থান ভেক্টর, যেখানে t হচ্ছে সময়। বস্তুটির বেগ কত?

Options:
ক. <span class="math-tex">\(2 \mathrm{t} \hat{\mathrm{i}}\)</span>
খ. <span class="math-tex">\(2 \hat{i}+6 t \hat{j}\)</span>
গ. <span class="math-tex">\(6 t \hat{j}\)</span>
ঘ. <span class="math-tex">\( 2 \hat{i}+3 t \hat{j}\)</span>


Correct Answer: খ

Solution:
\(\vec{r}=2 t \hat{i}+3 t^2 \hat{j} \Rightarrow \frac{d \bar{r}}{d t}=2 \hat{i}+6 t \hat{j}\)
