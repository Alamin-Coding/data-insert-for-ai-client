Question:
[math]ABCDEF[/math] একটি সুষম ষড়ভুজ। [math]\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AC}}[/math] [math]+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AD}}+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AE}}[/math] [math]+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AF}}=?[/math]

Options:


Correct Answer: A.

Solution:

[Image]
[math]|\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AF}}|[/math]
 [math]=2 \mathrm{AB} \cos \left(\frac{120^{\circ}}{2}\right)[/math]
 [math]=\mathrm{AB} ; \quad|\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AC}}+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AE}}|[/math]
 [math]=2 \mathrm{AC} \cos \left(\frac{60^{\circ}}{2}\right)[/math]
 [math]=\sqrt{3} \mathrm{AC}[/math]
 [math]\triangle A B C[/math]
 এ [math]\frac{A B}{\sin 30^{\circ}}[/math]
 [math]=\frac{A C}{\sin 120^{\circ}}[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow A B[/math]
 [math]=\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}} A C[/math]
আবার, [math]\triangle A C D[/math]
 এ [math]\frac{A C}{\sin 60^{\circ}}[/math]
 [math]=\frac{A D}{\sin 90^{\circ}}[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow A C[/math]
 [math]=\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} A D[/math]
 [math]\therefore \overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AC}}+\overrightarrow{mathrm{AD}}+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AE}}[/math]
 [math]+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AF}}[/math]
 [math]=(\mathrm{AB}+\sqrt{3} \mathrm{AC}[/math]
 [math]+\mathrm{AD}) \frac{\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AD}}}{\mathrm{AD}}[/math]
 [math]=\left(\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}} \cdot \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}+\sqrt{3} \cdot \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}+1\right) \overrightarrow{\mathrm{AD}}[/math]
 [math]=3 \mathrm{AD}[/math]
 [math]\text { Alternate: } \overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}+[/math]
 [math]\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AC}}[/math]
 [math]+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AD}}+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AE}}[/math]
 [math]+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AF}}[/math]
 [math]=\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}+(\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}[/math]
 [math]+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{BC}})[/math]
 [math]+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AD}}+(\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AD}}[/math]
 [math]+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{DE}})+(\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AE}}+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{EF}})[/math]
 [math]\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}[/math]
 [math]+(\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}[/math]
 [math]+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{BC}})[/math]
 [math]+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AD}}+(\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AD}}[/math]
 [math]+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{DE}})+[/math]
 [math](\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AD}}[/math]
 [math]+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{DE}}+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{EF}})[/math]
 [math]=3 \overrightarrow{\mathrm{AD}}[/math]
 [math]+2 \overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}[/math]
 [math]+2 \overrightarrow{\mathrm{DE}}[/math]
 [math]+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{BC}}[/math]
 [math]+\overrightarrow{\mathrm{EF}}[/math]
 [math]=3 \overrightarrow{\mathrm{AD}}[/math]
কিন্তু [math]\mathrm{AB} \| \mathrm{DE}, \mathrm{AB}=\mathrm{DE}[/math]
 কারণ সুষম ষড়ভুজ বলে [math]\therefore \overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}[/math]
 [math]=-\overrightarrow{\mathrm{DE}}[/math]
 এবং [math]\overrightarrow{\mathrm{BC}}[/math]
 [math]=-\overrightarrow{\mathrm{EF}}][/math]


Explanation:
ব্যাখ্যা: কেন্দ্রীয় বিন্দু O-ধরে প্রতিটি শীর্ষের অবস্থান ভেক্টর নিয়ে [math]\vec a=\vec{OA},\ \vec b=\vec{OB},\dots,\vec f=\vec{OF}[/math] ধরা যায় এবং ছয়টি শীর্ষের যোগ শূন্য: [math]\vec a+\vec b+\vec c+\vec d+\vec e+\vec f=\vec0[/math]. তাই
[math]\vec{AB}+\vec{AC}+\vec{AD}+\vec{AE}+\vec{AF}=(\vec b+\vec c+\vec d+\vec e+\vec f)-5\vec a=-6\vec a[/math],
আর [math]\vec{AD}=\vec d-\vec a=-2\vec a, কাজেই সমষ্টি = [math]-6\vec a=3(\vec d-\vec a)=3\vec{AD}. অতএব (A) সঠিক।
