Question:
 যদি  [math]\overrightarrow{\mathbf{A}}=\hat{\mathbf{i}}+2 \hat{\mathbf{j}}+\mathrm{p} \hat{\mathbf{k}}[/math]  এবং [math]\overrightarrow{\mathbf{B}}=3 \hat{\mathbf{i}}-\hat{\mathbf{j}}+2 \hat{\mathbf{k}}[/math]  হয় এবং [math](\overrightarrow{\mathbf{A}}+\overrightarrow{\mathbf{B}})[/math]  এবং [math](\overrightarrow{\mathbf{A}}-\overrightarrow{\mathbf{B}})[/math]   ভেক্টর দুইটি পরস্পর লম্ব হলে নিচের কোনটি সত্য?

Options:
ক. <span class="math-tex">\(\mathrm{p}=-3\)</span>
খ. <span class="math-tex">\( p=2\)</span>
গ. <span class="math-tex">\( \mathrm{p}=9\)</span>
ঘ. <span class="math-tex">\(p=-5\)</span>


Correct Answer: ক

Solution:
\(\begin{aligned}&amp; \text { () }|\overrightarrow{\mathrm{A}}|=|\overrightarrow{\mathrm{B}}| \Rightarrow \sqrt{1+4+\mathrm{p}^2}=\sqrt{9+1+4} \\&amp; \Rightarrow \mathrm{p}^2+5=14 \Rightarrow \mathrm{p}^2=9 \Rightarrow \mathrm{P}= \pm 3\end{aligned}\)

Question:
[math]\overrightarrow{\mathrm{u}}=2 \hat{\mathbf{i}}+3 \hat{\mathbf{j}}-\hat{\mathrm{k}}[/math] এবং [math]\overrightarrow{\mathrm{v}}=6 \hat{\mathbf{i}}+\alpha \hat{\mathbf{j}}-3 \hat{\mathbf{k}}[/math]   ভেক্টর দুইটি পরস্পর সমান্তরাল হলে, x এর মান কত? 

Options:
ক. <span class="math-tex">\(3\)</span>
খ. <span class="math-tex">\(-3\)</span>
গ. <span class="math-tex">\( 9\)</span>
ঘ. <span class="math-tex">\(-9\)</span>

\(\frac{2}{6}=\frac{3}{\alpha}=\frac{-1}{-3} \Rightarrow \alpha=3 \times 3=9\)

Question:

Options:
ক. <span class="math-tex">\(-1\)</span>
খ. <span class="math-tex">\(2\)</span>
গ. <span class="math-tex">\(3\)</span>
ঘ. <span class="math-tex">\(-5\)</span>

Solution:
\(\frac{2}{10}=\frac{\mathrm{m}}{-5}=\frac{-3}{-15} \Rightarrow \frac{2}{10}=\frac{\mathrm{m}}{-5} \Rightarrow 10 \mathrm{~m}=-10 \therefore \mathrm{m}=-1\)
