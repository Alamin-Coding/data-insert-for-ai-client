Question:
[math]\vec{P} \cdot \vec{Q}=4 \sqrt{3}[/math] এবং [math]|\vec{P} \times \vec{Q}|=4[/math]  হলে [math]\vec{P} В \vec{Q}[/math]  ভেক্টর দুইটির মধ্যবর্তী

কোণ কত? 

Options:
ক. <span class="math-tex">\(30^{\circ}\)</span>
খ. <span class="math-tex">\(60^{\circ}\)</span>
গ. <span class="math-tex">\(120^{\circ}\)</span>
ঘ. <span class="math-tex">\(150^{\circ}\)</span>


Correct Answer: ক

Solution:
\(\vec{P} \cdot \vec{Q}=4 \sqrt{3} \Rightarrow P Q \cos \theta=4 \sqrt{3}\)আবার,\(​​​​​​​ |\vec{P} \times \vec{Q}|=4 \Rightarrow P Q \sin \theta=4\\\therefore \frac{P Q \sin \theta}{P Q \cos \theta}=\frac{4}{4 \sqrt{3}}\\ \Rightarrow \tan \theta=\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}=\tan 30^{\circ} \Rightarrow \theta=30^{\circ}\)

দুটি [math]\vec{a}-\vec{b}[/math]  এবং [math]\vec{a}+\vec{b}[/math] ক্টরের গুণন (ক্রস গুণন)-এর মান হলো-।

Options:
ক. <span class="math-tex">\(\vec{a} \times \vec{b}\)</span>
খ. <span class="math-tex">\(2(\vec{a} \times \vec{b})\)</span>
গ. <span class="math-tex">\( \vec{b} \times \vec{a}\)</span>
ঘ. <span class="math-tex">\( \mathrm{a}^2-\mathrm{b}^2\)</span>


Correct Answer: খ

Solution:
\(\begin{aligned}&amp; (\vec{a}-\vec{b}) \times(\vec{a}+\vec{b})=\vec{a} \times \vec{a}+\vec{a} \times \vec{b}-\vec{b} \times \vec{a}-\vec{b} \times \vec{b} \\&amp; =0+\vec{a} \times \vec{b}+\vec{a} \times \vec{b}-0=2(\vec{a} \times \vec{b})\end{aligned}\)

Question:
[math]\overline{\mathbf{A}}+\overline{\mathbf{B}}=\overline{\mathbf{A}}-\overline{\mathbf{B}}[/math] হলে  [math]\overline{\mathbf{A}}\ ও\  \overline{\mathbf{B}}[/math] ভেক্টরদ্বয়ের  মধ্যবর্তী কোণ কত?

Options:
ক. <span class="math-tex">\(0\)</span>
খ. <span class="math-tex">\(90^{\circ}\)</span>
গ. <span class="math-tex">\(120^{\circ}\)</span>
ঘ. <span class="math-tex">\(180^{\circ}\)</span>


Correct Answer: খ

Solution:
\(\begin{aligned}&amp; \text { B } \mathrm{B} \text { } \overline{\mathrm{A}}+\overline{\mathrm{B}}=\overline{\mathrm{A}}-\overline{\mathrm{B}} \\&amp; \Rightarrow \overline{\mathrm{A}} \cdot(\overline{\mathrm{A}}+\overline{\mathrm{B}})=\overline{\mathrm{A}} \cdot(\overline{\mathrm{A}}-\overline{\mathrm{B}}) \\&amp; \Rightarrow \overline{\mathrm{A}} \cdot \overline{\mathrm{A}}+\overline{\mathrm{A}} \cdot \overline{\mathrm{B}}=\overline{\mathrm{A}} \cdot \overline{\mathrm{A}}-\overline{\mathrm{A}} \cdot \overline{\mathrm{B}} \\&amp; \Rightarrow 2 \overline{\mathrm{A}} \cdot \overline{\mathrm{B}}=0 \Rightarrow \overline{\mathrm{A}} \cdot \overline{\mathrm{B}}=0 \Rightarrow \mathrm{AB} \cos \theta=0 \\&amp; \Rightarrow \cos \theta=0 \Rightarrow \cos \theta=\cos 90^{\circ} \therefore \theta=90^{\circ}\end{aligned}\)
