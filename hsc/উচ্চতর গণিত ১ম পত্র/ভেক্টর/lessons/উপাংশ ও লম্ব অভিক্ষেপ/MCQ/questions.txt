Question:

Options:
A. [math]1[/math]
B. [math]2[/math]
C. [math]3[/math]
D. [math]4[/math]
E. [math]5[/math]


Correct Answer: E.[math]5[/math]

Solution:
[math]\overrightarrow{\mathrm{b}}[/math]
 এর উপর [math]\overrightarrow{\mathbf{a}}[/math]
 এর অভিক্ষেপ [math]=\frac{\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b}}{|\vec{b}|}=\frac{2 \lambda+6+12}{\sqrt{4+36+9}}=\frac{2 \lambda+18}{7}[/math]
প্রশ্নমতে [math]\frac{2 \lambda+18}{7}=4 \Rightarrow 2 \lambda+18=28 \Rightarrow \lambda=5[/math]
