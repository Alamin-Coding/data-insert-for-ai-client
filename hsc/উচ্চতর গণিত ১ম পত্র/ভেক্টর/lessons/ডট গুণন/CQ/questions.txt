Question:
নিচের তথ্যের আলোকে প্রশ্নের উত্তর দাও:

[math]\mathbf{P}=\hat{i}-3 \hat{j}+5 \hat{k}[/math] এবং [math]\mathbf{Q}=3 \hat{\mathrm{i}}-2 \hat{\mathrm{j}}+4 \hat{\mathrm{k}}[/math] দুটি একই সমতলে অবস্থিত ভেক্টর।

[math]|\mathrm{PQ}|=[/math] কত?

Options:


Correct Answer: D.

Solution:
[math]\overrightarrow{P Q}[/math]
 [math]=\overrightarrow{O Q}-\overrightarrow{O P}[/math]
 [math]=(3 \hat{i}[/math]
 [math]-2 \hat{\mathrm{j}}[/math]
 [math]+4 \hat{\mathrm{k}})-(\hat{\mathrm{i}}[/math]
 [math]-3 \hat{\mathrm{j}}[/math]
 [math]+5 \hat{\mathrm{k}})[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow 2 \hat{\mathrm{i}}[/math]
 [math]+\hat{\mathrm{j}}-\hat{\mathrm{k}}[/math]
 [math]\therefore |\mathrm{PQ}|[/math]
 [math]=\sqrt{2^{2}+1^{2}+(-1)^{2}}[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow \sqrt{4+1+1}[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow \sqrt{6}[/math]


Explanation:
ব্যাখ্যা: [math]\mathbf{PQ}=\mathbf{Q}-\mathbf{P}=(3-1)\hat{i}+(-2-(-3))\hat{j}+(4-5)\hat{k}=2\hat{i}+1\hat{j}-1\hat{k}[/math]. তাই [math]|\mathbf{PQ}|=\sqrt{2^2+1^2+(-1)^2}=\sqrt{6}[/math], ফলে উত্তরটি D সঠিক।

Question:
ভেক্টর দুইটির অন্তর্গত কোণ [math]A=\sqrt{2} \cos \alpha \hat{i}-\sqrt{2} \sin \alpha \hat{j} \quad B[/math] [math]=-\sqrt{2} \cos \alpha \hat{j}[/math] [math]+\sqrt{2} \sin \alpha \hat{k}(0<\alpha<\pi / 2)[/math]

Options:


Correct Answer: D.

Solution:
[math]\cos \theta=\frac{\vec{A} \cdot \bar{B}}{A \cdot B}[/math]
 [math]=\frac{(\sqrt{2} \cos \alpha \hat{i}-\sqrt{2} \sin \alpha \hat{j})(-\sqrt{2} \cos \alpha \hat{j}+\sqrt{2} \sin \alpha \hat{k})}{\sqrt{(\sqrt{2} \cos \alpha)^{2}+(-\sqrt{2} \sin \alpha)^{2}} \cdot \sqrt{(-\sqrt{2} \cos \alpha)^{2}+(\sqrt{2} \sin \alpha)^{2}}}[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow\frac{2 \sin \alpha \cos \alpha}{\sqrt{2} \cdot \sqrt{2}}[/math]
 [math][\hat{i} . \hat{j}=0 ; \hat{i} \cdot \hat{k}=0 ; \hat{j} \cdot \hat{k}=0][/math]
 [math]=\sin \alpha \cos \alpha \quad[/math]
 [math]\therefore \theta[/math]
 [math]=\cos ^{-1}(\sin \alpha \cos \alpha)[/math]


Explanation:
[math]\mathbf{A}\cdot\mathbf{B}= (\sqrt2\cos\alpha)\cdot0+(-\sqrt2\sin\alpha)(-\sqrt2\cos\alpha)+0\cdot(\sqrt2\sin\alpha)=2\sin\alpha\cos\alpha=\sin2\alpha[/math].  
[math]|\mathbf{A}|=|\mathbf{B}|=\sqrt{2}[/math], তাই [math]\cos\theta=\dfrac{\mathbf{A}\cdot\mathbf{B}}{|\mathbf{A}||\mathbf{B}|}=\dfrac{\sin2\alpha}{2}[/math].  
[math]00[/math], সুতরাং [math]\theta=\cos^{-1}\!\left(\dfrac{\sin2\alpha}{2}\right)[/math] যা অপশন ডি-এর সাথে মেলে।

Correct Answer: A.

Solution:

Question:

Correct Answer: C.

Solution:
[math]|\overrightarrow{\mathrm{AB}}|=|\hat{\mathrm{i}}[/math]
 [math]+2 \hat{\mathrm{j}}-\hat{\mathrm{k}}-\hat{\mathrm{i}}[/math]

 [math]=\sqrt{13}[/math]
