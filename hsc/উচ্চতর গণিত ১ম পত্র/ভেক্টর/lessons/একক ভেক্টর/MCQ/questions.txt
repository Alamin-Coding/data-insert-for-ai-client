Question:

Options:
ক. [math]\frac{1}{3}(\hat{i}+2 \hat{j}-2 \hat{k})[/math]
খ. [math]\frac{1}{3}(\hat{i}-2 \hat{j}-2 \hat{k})[/math]
গ. [math]\frac{1}{3}(\hat{i}+2 \hat{j}+2 \hat{k})[/math]

Correct Answer: A.[math]\frac{1}{3}(\hat{i}+2 \hat{j}-2 \hat{k})[/math]

 [math]=\hat{i}+2 \hat{j}-2 \hat{k}[/math]
 [math]\therefore \overrightarrow{\mathrm{PQ}}[/math]
 ভেক্টরের সমান্তরাল একক ভেক্টর[math]\Rightarrow \frac{\hat{\mathrm{i}}+2 \hat{\mathrm{j}}-2 \hat{\mathrm{k}}}{\sqrt{1^{2}+2^{2}+(-2)^{2}}}[/math]
 [math]=\frac{1}{3}(\hat{\mathrm{i}}+2 \hat{\mathrm{j}}-2 \hat{\mathrm{k}})[/math]
