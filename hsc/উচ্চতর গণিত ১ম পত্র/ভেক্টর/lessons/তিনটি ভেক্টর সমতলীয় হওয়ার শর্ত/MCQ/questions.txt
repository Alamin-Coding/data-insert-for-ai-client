Question:
[math]\overrightarrow{\mathbf{A}}[/math] ও [math]\overrightarrow{\mathrm{C}}[/math]  দুটি ভেক্টর হলে [math]\overrightarrow{\mathbf{A}} \cdot(\overrightarrow{\mathbf{A}} \times \overrightarrow{\mathbf{C}})[/math] এর মান কত? 

Options:
ক. <span class="math-tex">\( \vec{A}\)</span>
খ. <span class="math-tex">\( \overrightarrow{\mathrm{C}}\)</span>
গ. <span class="math-tex">\(\mathrm{A}^2\)</span>
ঘ. <span class="math-tex">\(0\)</span>


Correct Answer: ঘ

Solution:
\(\vec{A} \cdot(\vec{A} \times \vec{C})=(\vec{A} \times \vec{A}) \cdot \vec{C}=\overrightarrow{0} \cdot \vec{C}=0\)

Question:
[math]\overrightarrow{\mathbf{A}}=2 \hat{\mathbf{i}}+\hat{\mathbf{j}}-\hat{\mathbf{k}}, \overrightarrow{\mathbf{B}}=\hat{\mathbf{i}}+2 \hat{\mathbf{j}}+3 \hat{\mathbf{k}}[/math]এবং [math]\overrightarrow{\mathbf{C}}=2 \hat{\mathbf{i}}-3 \hat{\mathbf{j}}+a \hat{\mathbf{k}}[/math]  ভেক্টরতিনটি সমতলীয় হলে   a এর মান- 

Options:
ক. <span class="math-tex">\( \frac{-31}{5}\)</span>
খ. <span class="math-tex">\(\frac{-31}{4}\)</span>
গ. <span class="math-tex">\(\frac{-31}{3}\)</span>
ঘ. <span class="math-tex">\(\frac{-31}{6}\)</span>


Correct Answer: গ

Solution:
সমতলীয় হলে,\(\left|\begin{array}{ccc}2 &amp; 1 &amp; -1 \\ 1 &amp; 2 &amp; 3 \\ 2 &amp; -3 &amp; a\end{array}\right|=0\)\(\begin{aligned}&amp; \Rightarrow 2(2 a+9)-1(a-6)-1(-3-4)=0 \\&amp; \Rightarrow 4 a+18-a+6+7=0 \Rightarrow 3 a=-31 \therefore a=-\frac{31}{3}\end{aligned}\)

Question:
তিনটি ভেক্টর [math]\overline{\mathrm{A}}, \overline{\mathrm{B}}[/math] এবং [math]\overline{\mathrm{C}}[/math]  সমতলীয় হওয়ার শর্ত হল- 

Options:
ক. <span class="math-tex">\(\overline{\mathrm{A}} \cdot(\overline{\mathrm{B}} \times \overline{\mathrm{C}})=0\)</span>
খ. <span class="math-tex">\( \bar{A} \cdot \bar{B} \cdot \bar{C}=0\)</span>
গ. <span class="math-tex">\( \overline{\mathrm{A}} \times(\overline{\mathrm{B}} \times \overline{\mathrm{C}})=0\)</span>
ঘ. <span class="math-tex">\( (\overline{\mathrm{A}} \cdot \overline{\mathrm{B}}) \times \overline{\mathrm{C}}=0\)</span>


Correct Answer: ক

Solution:
\((\vec{A} \times \vec{B}) \cdot \vec{C}=\vec{A} \cdot(\vec{B} \times \vec{C})=\vec{B} \cdot(\vec{C} \times \vec{A})=0\)

Question:
'a' এর কোন মানের জন্য [math]\overrightarrow{\mathbf{A}}=2 \hat{\mathbf{i}}+3 \hat{\mathbf{j}}+4 \hat{\mathbf{k}}, \overrightarrow{\mathrm{B}}=3 \hat{\mathbf{i}}+4 \hat{\mathbf{j}}+a \hat{\mathbf{k}}[/math]  ও [math]\overrightarrow{\mathbf{C}}=\mathrm{ai}+7 \hat{\mathbf{i}}+9 \hat{\mathbf{k}}[/math] সমতলীয় হবে? 

Options:
ক. <span class="math-tex">\(2\)</span>
খ. <span class="math-tex">\( -2\)</span>
গ. <span class="math-tex">\(3\)</span>
ঘ. <span class="math-tex">\(5\)</span>


Correct Answer: ঘ

Solution:
সমতলীয় হলে,\(​​​​​​​\left|\begin{array}{lll}2 &amp; 3 &amp; 4 \\ 3 &amp; 4 &amp; \mathrm{a} \\ \mathrm{a} &amp; 7 &amp; 9\end{array}\right|=0\\\begin{aligned}&amp; \Rightarrow 2(36-7 a)-3\left(27-a^2\right)+4(21-4 a)=0 \\&amp; \Rightarrow 3 a^2-30 a+75=0 \Rightarrow a^2-10 a+25=0 \Rightarrow(a-5)^2=0 \therefore a=5\end{aligned}\)
