Options:
ক. [math]\frac{2 \pi}{3}[/math]
খ. [math]\frac{\pi}{10}[/math]
গ. [math]\frac{\pi}{3}[/math]
ঘ. [math]\frac{\pi}{5}[/math]


Correct Answer: C.[math]\frac{\pi}{3}[/math]

Solution:
কোন একক ভেক্টর অক্ষত্রয়ের সাথে [math]\theta_{x}, \theta_{y}[/math]
 ও [math]\theta_{z}[/math]
 কোণ উৎপন্ন করলে,[math]\begin{array}{l}\cos ^{2} \theta_{x}+\cos ^{2} \theta_{y}+\cos ^{2} \theta_{z}=1 \\\Rightarrow \cos ^{2} \frac{\pi}{3}+\cos ^{2} \frac{\pi}{4}+\cos ^{2} \theta_{z}=1 \\\Rightarrow \frac{1}{4}+\frac{1}{2}+\cos ^{2} \theta_{z}=1 \\\Rightarrow \cos ^{2} \theta_{z}=1-\frac{3}{4}=\frac{1}{4} \\\Rightarrow \cos \theta_{z}=\frac{1}{2} \\\therefore \theta_{z}=\cos ^{-1} \frac{1}{2}=\frac{\pi}{3}\end{array}[/math]

 [math]\Rightarrow 4 A \cdot B=0[/math]
 [math]\therefore \mathbf{A}[/math]
 ও [math]B[/math]
