Question:
[math]2 \hat{\mathbf{i}}+\mathbf{2} \hat{\mathbf{j}}[/math] ভেক্টরটি  X ক্ষের সাথে কত ডিগ্রি কোণ তৈরি করে? 

Options:
ক. <span class="math-tex">\(30^{\circ}\)</span>
খ. <span class="math-tex">\(45^{\circ}\)</span>
গ. <span class="math-tex">\( 90^{\circ}\)</span>
ঘ. <span class="math-tex">\(180^{\circ}\)</span>


Correct Answer: খ

Solution:
কোণ\(\theta=\cos ^{-1} \frac{(2 \hat{i}+2 \hat{j}) \cdot \hat{i}}{\sqrt{2^2+2^2}}=\cos ^{-1} \frac{2}{2 \sqrt{2}}=\cos ^{-1} \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}=45^{\circ}\)

Question:
[math]\gamma[/math]এর কোণ মানের জন্য [math]\vec{A}=\gamma \hat{i}+4 \hat{j}+3 \hat{k}[/math] ভেক্টরটি X অক্ষের সাথে 30° কোণ উৎপন্ন করে?

Options:
ক. <span class="math-tex">\(3 \sqrt{3}\)</span>
খ. <span class="math-tex">\( 5 \sqrt{3}\)</span>
গ. <span class="math-tex">\(\sqrt{3}\)</span>
ঘ. <span class="math-tex">\(\sqrt{5}\)</span>


Correct Answer: খ

Solution:
\(\overrightarrow{\mathrm{A}}=\gamma \hat{\mathrm{i}}+4 \hat{\mathrm{j}}+3 \hat{\mathrm{k}}, \mathrm{x}\)অক্ষে ভেক্টর\(\hat{\mathrm{i}}\)প্রশ্নমতে,\(\begin{aligned}&amp; \text { , } \cos 30^{\circ}=\frac{\gamma \times 1}{\sqrt{\gamma^2+16+9} \cdot \sqrt{1}} \\&amp; \Rightarrow \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}=\frac{\gamma}{\sqrt{\gamma^2+25}} \Rightarrow \frac{3}{4}=\frac{\gamma^2}{\gamma^2+25} \\&amp; \Rightarrow 4 \gamma^2-3 \gamma^2=75 \Rightarrow \gamma= \pm 5 \sqrt{3}\end{aligned}\)
