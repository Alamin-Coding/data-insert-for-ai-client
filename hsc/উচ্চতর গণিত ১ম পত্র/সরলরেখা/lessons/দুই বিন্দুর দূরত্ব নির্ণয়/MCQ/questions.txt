Question:

Options:
ক. 14
খ. 12
গ. 8
ঘ. 10


Correct Answer: ঘ

Solution:
মূলবিন্দু হতে সমদূরবর্তী রেখাদ্বয় হলে[math]\begin{array}{l} \pm \frac{4.0+3.0-\mathrm{c}}{\sqrt{4^{2}+3^{2}}}= \pm \frac{12.0-5.0-2(\mathrm{c}+3)}{\sqrt{12^{2}+5^{2}}} \Rightarrow \pm\frac{\mathrm{c}}{5}= \pm \frac{2(\mathrm{c}+3)}{13} \\\Rightarrow 13 \mathrm{c}= \pm(10 \mathrm{c}-30) \Rightarrow \mathrm{c}=10,-\frac{30}{23}\end{array}[/math]

Question:

খ. 6,-2
গ. -6,2
ঘ. 6,2


Correct Answer: ক

Solution:
শর্তমতে,[math]\left|\frac{-\mathrm{k}}{\sqrt{4^{2}+3^{2}}}\right|=\left|\frac{-2(\mathrm{k}+3)}{\sqrt{4^{2}+3^{2}}}\right|[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow \pm k=2(k+3) \\\Rightarrow 2 k+6= \pm k \\\Rightarrow 2 k \pm k=-6 \\\Rightarrow k=-2,-6[/math]
