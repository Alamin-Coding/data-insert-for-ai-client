[math]{ }^{\mathrm{n}} \mathrm{P}_2=42[/math] হলে, n এর মান কত?

Options:
ক. <span class="math-tex">\( 5\)</span>
খ. <span class="math-tex">\(6\)</span>
গ. <span class="math-tex">\(7\)</span>
ঘ. <span class="math-tex">\(8\)</span>


Correct Answer: গ

Solution:
\(^n P_2=42 \Rightarrow n(n-1)=42 \Rightarrow n^2-n-42=0\)\(\begin{aligned}&amp; \Rightarrow \mathrm{n}^2-7 \mathrm{n}+6 \mathrm{n}-42=0\\ &amp;\Rightarrow \mathrm{n}(\mathrm{n}-7)+6(\mathrm{n}-7)=0 \\&amp; \Rightarrow(\mathrm{n}-7)(\mathrm{n}+6)=0\\ &amp;\therefore \mathrm{n}=7, \mathrm{n} 
eq-6\end{aligned}\)
