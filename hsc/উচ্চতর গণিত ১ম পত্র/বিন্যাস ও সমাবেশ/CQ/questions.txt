Question:
[math]{ }^{n} P_{4}=6^{n} P_{3}[/math] হলে [math]n[/math] এর মান কত?

Options:


Correct Answer: ক

Solution:
[math]{ }^{n} P_{4}=6 \times{ }^{n} P_{3}[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow \frac{\ln }{\ln -4}=6[/math]
 [math]\times \frac{\ln }{\ln -3}[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow \frac{1}{(n-4) !}[/math]
 [math]=\frac{6}{(n-3)(n-4) !}[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow n-3=6[/math]
 [math]\therefore n=9[/math]


Explanation:
কারণ [math]{}^{n}P_{4}=n(n-1)(n-2)(n-3)[/math] এবং ধরে নিতে হবে ডানপাশটি [math]{}^{6}P_{3}=6\cdot5\cdot4=120[/math]; তাই [math]n(n-1)(n-2)(n-3)=120[/math]। পরীক্ষা করলে [math]n=5[/math] হলে [math]5\cdot4\cdot3\cdot2=120[/math], অতএব [math]n=5[/math]।

eq 1[/math]

Question:
যদি [math]{ }^{n} P_{r}=240[/math] এবং [math]{ }^{n} C_{r}=120[/math] হয়, তবে n ও r এর মান নির্ণয় কর।

Options:


Correct Answer: C.

Solution:
[math]{ }^{n} P_{r}=\frac{n !}{(n-r) !}=240[/math]
 [math]\text { and }{ }^{n} C_{r}[/math]
 [math]=\frac{n !}{r !(n-r) !}[/math]
 [math]=120[/math]
 [math]\mathrm{r} !=2 \text { or } r=2 \quad[/math]
 [math]\therefore \mathrm{n}^{2}-\mathrm{n}=240[/math]
 [math]\quad \therefore(\mathrm{n}-16)(\mathrm{n}+15)=0 \quad[/math]
 [math]\therefore \mathrm{n}=16[/math]


Explanation:
ব্যাখ্যা: কারণ [math]{}^{n}P_{r}={}^{n}C_{r}\cdot r![/math], তাই [math]240=120\cdot r!\Rightarrow r!=2\Rightarrow r=2[/math]. এরপর [math]{}^{n}C_{2}=120\Rightarrow \frac{n(n-1)}{2}=120\Rightarrow n(n-1)=240\Rightarrow n^2-n-240=0[/math], সমাধানে ধনাত্মক মূলটি [math]n=(1+31)/2=16[/math]; অতএব [math]n=16,\; r=2[/math].

Solution:
[math]{ }^{14} \mathrm{P}_{\mathrm{r}}={ }^{14} \mathrm{C}_{\mathrm{r}}[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow{ }^{14} \mathrm{C}_{\mathrm{r}} \cdot \mathrm{r} ![/math]
 [math]={ }^{14} \mathrm{C}_{\mathrm{r}}[/math]

Question:
[math]{ }^{n} C_{5}={ }^{n} C_{7}[/math] হলে [math]{ }^{n} C_{9}[/math] এর মান কত?

Options:


Correct Answer: B.

Solution:
[math]{ }^{n} C_{5}={ }^{n} C_{7}[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow{ }^{n} C_{n-5}={ }^{n} C_{7}[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow n-5=7[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow n=12[/math]
 [math]\therefore{ }^{n} C_{9}={ }^{12} C_{9}=220[/math]


Explanation:
