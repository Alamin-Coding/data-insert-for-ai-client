Question:
[math]x ^ 2 + y ^ 2 - 2x + 4y = 0 = x ^ 2 + y ^ 2 - 3x + 5y + 2[/math] বৃত্তদ্বয়ের সাধারণ জ্যা- এর দৈর্ঘ্য বের করো।

Options:
ক. [math]3 \sqrt{2}[/math]
খ. [math]\sqrt{2}[/math]
গ. [math]\sqrt{6}[/math]
ঘ. [math]2 \sqrt{3}[/math]


Correct Answer: ক

Solution:
[math]x^{2}+y^{2}-2 x+4 y=0 \ldots (i)[/math]
 [math]x^{2}+y^{2}-3 x+5 y+2=0 \ldots . .(\mathrm{ii})[/math]
(i) নং এর কেন্দ্র = (1, - 2) এবং [math]r=\sqrt{1^{2}+4-0}=\sqrt{5}[/math]
 [math]\begin{array}{l}\text { (i) }- \text { (ii) } \Rightarrow x-y-2=0 \\\therefore d=\left|\frac{1+2-2}{\sqrt{1^{2}+1^{2}}}\right|=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\end{array}[/math]
জ্যা এর দৈর্ঘ্য [math]=2 \sqrt{r^{2}-d^{2}}=2 \sqrt{(\sqrt{5})^{2}-\left(\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\right)^{2}}=3 \sqrt{2}[/math]
