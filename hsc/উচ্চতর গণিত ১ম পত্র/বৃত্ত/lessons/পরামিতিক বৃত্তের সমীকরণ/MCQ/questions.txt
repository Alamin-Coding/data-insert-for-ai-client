Question:
$\mathbf{r}=\mathbf{a} \sin \theta$ এর কার্তেসীয় সমীকরণ কোনটি?

Options:
A. [math]a x^{2}+y^{2}-y=0[/math]
B. [math]x^{2}+y^{2}+a y=0[/math]
C. [math]x^{2}+y^{2}-a y=0[/math]
D. [math]x^{2}+a y^{2}+y=0[/math]


Correct Answer: গ

Solution:
[math]r=a \sin \theta \\\Rightarrow r^{2}=a r \sin \theta \\\Rightarrow x^{2}+y^{2}-a y=0[/math]
