Question:
[math]\lim _{x \rightarrow 0} \frac{\cos x-1}{x^{2}}[/math] এর মান কোনটি?

Options:
A. -8
B. [math]\frac{1}{2}[/math]
C. [math]\frac{1}{2}[/math]
D. 8


Correct Answer: খ

Solution:
[math]\lim _{x \rightarrow 0} \frac{\cos x-1}{x^{2}}[/math]
 [math]=\lim _{x \rightarrow 0} \frac{-\sin x}{2 x}=-\frac{1}{2} \times \lim _{x \rightarrow 0} \frac{\sin x}{x}=-\frac{1}{2} \times 1=-\frac{1}{2}[/math]
