Question:
[math]y=\cot ^{-1} \sqrt{\frac{1+\cos x}{1-\cos x}}[/math] হলে, [math]\frac{d y}{d x}=[/math]কত ?

Options:


Correct Answer: B.

Solution:
[math]\sqrt{\frac{1+\cos x}{1-\cos x}}[/math]
 [math]=\sqrt{\frac{2 \cos ^{2} \frac{x}{2}}{2 \sin ^{2} \frac{x}{2}}}[/math]
 [math]\Rightarrow \sqrt{\cot ^{2} \frac{x}{2}}[/math]
 [math]=\cot \frac{x}{2}[/math]
 [math]\therefore y=\cot ^{-1}\left(\cot \frac{x}{2}\right)[/math]
 [math]=\frac{x}{2}[/math]
 [math]\therefore \frac{d y}{d x}=\frac{1}{2}[/math]


Explanation:
ব্যাখ্যা: [math]\tan\frac{x}{2}=\sqrt{\frac{1-\cos x}{1+\cos x}}[/math] থেকে [math]\sqrt{\frac{1+\cos x}{1-\cos x}}=\cot\frac{x}{2}[/math] হয়, তাই [math]y=\cot^{-1}\!\left(\cot\frac{x}{2}\right)=\frac{x}{2}[/math] (প্রধান শাখায়)। ফলে [math]\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{1}{2}[/math].

Question:
[math]\lim _{x \rightarrow \infty} x \sin \left(\frac{2}{x}\right)=?[/math]

Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:
[math]\begin{array}{l}\lim _{x \rightarrow \infty} \frac{x \sin _{x}^{2}}{x}=\lim _{x \rightarrow \infty} \frac{\sin \frac{2}{x}}{\frac{2}{x}} \times 2 \\=\lim _{\theta \rightarrow 0} \frac{\sin \theta}{\theta} \times 2 \\=1 \times 2-2\end{array}[/math]

Explanation:
As [math]x\to\infty[/math], [math]\frac{2}{x}\to0[/math], and using the small-angle limit [math]\lim_{t\to0}\frac{\sin t}{t}=1[/math] we get [math]\sin\left(\frac{2}{x}\right)\sim\frac{2}{x}[/math], so [math]\lim_{x\to\infty}x\sin\left(\frac{2}{x}\right)=\lim_{x\to\infty}x\cdot\frac{2}{x}=2[/math].

Question:
[math]\lim _{x \rightarrow \infty} x \sin \left(\frac{2}{x}\right)=?[/math]

Options:


Correct Answer: 

Solution:
[math]\lim _{x \rightarrow \infty} x \sin \frac{2}{x}=\lim _{x \rightarrow \infty} \frac{\sin \frac{2}{x}}{\frac{2}{x}} \times 2[/math]
 [math]\begin{array}{l}=\lim _{\theta \rightarrow 0} \frac{\sin \theta}{\theta} \times 2 \\=1 \times 2=2\end{array}[/math]


Explanation:
Let [math]t=\tfrac{2}{x}[/math], so as [math]x\to\infty[/math] we have [math]t\to 0[/math] and [math]x\sin\left(\tfrac{2}{x}\right)=2\frac{\sin t}{t}[/math]. Since [math]\lim_{t\to 0}\frac{\sin t}{t}=1[/math], the given limit equals [math]2[/math].
