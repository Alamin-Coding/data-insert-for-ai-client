Question:
Given recent emphasis on integrating Bangla and English general-knowledge content for HSC students, which study approach best combines bilingual comprehension and long-term retention?

Options:
a. Study general-knowledge topics only in Bangla to avoid confusion
b. Translate materials between Bangla and English and write bilingual summaries
c. Focus solely on English textbooks to master technical terms
d. Memorize facts in one language without practicing translation


Correct Answer: b

Solution:


Explanation:
Option b is best because translating materials and writing bilingual summaries forces active processing, links technical terms and meanings across both languages, and builds clearer conceptual understanding. This bilingual practice improves comprehension and recall, supporting longer-term retention better than single-language study or rote memorization.

Question:
If heavy floods across Bangladesh disrupt transport on HSC exam days, which action is most likely by authorities based on recent practices?

Options:
a. Postpone the exams to later dates
b. Cancel the exams for the entire year
c. Shift all exams to online mode immediately
d. Require affected students to travel to other districts to sit the exams


Correct Answer: a

Solution:


Explanation:
ব্যাখ্যা: বন্যা বা পরিবহন বিঘ্ন হলে কর্তৃপক্ষ সাধারণত পরীক্ষার তারিখ পেছিয়ে দেয় যাতে পরীক্ষার্থীরা নিরাপদে অংশ নিতে পারে, তাই ক সঠিক। সম্পূর্ণ বাতিল করা, হঠাৎ অনলাইনে নেয়া বা সবাইকে অন্যান্য জেলায় পাঠানো বাস্তবে কমই করা হয় এবং তা কার্যকর বা ন্যায্য নয়।
